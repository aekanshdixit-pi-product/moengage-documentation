# Introduction

In today's fast-paced digital landscape, marketers need a constant stream of high-quality, engaging visuals to capture audience attention. Merlin AI Designer allows you to skillfully generate images based on specific, detailed descriptions.

<Info>
The accuracy and effectiveness of generated images heavily on the level of detail provided. Using ambiguous terms or vague descriptions may lead to unexpected or undesired results. Therefore, for optimal outcomes, ensure that your descriptions are clear, precise, and packed with relevant details.
</Info>

## Example

**❌ A bad image description:**

*A surprise book. You must add a banner text: "Unlock a little magic" and CTA "Shop now".*

**✅ A good image description:**

*A gleaming, translucent gadget with a bubbling screensaver in a Y2K aesthetic. Cybernetic grid background. Add a message for Banner: "Future-proof your tech." And CTA: "Upgrade today."*

# Best Practices

Merlin AI is a powerful tool, but it's still evolving. Merlin AI can sometimes miss or misinterpret parts of an image description. MoEngage encourages you to review your prompts and the resulting output.
