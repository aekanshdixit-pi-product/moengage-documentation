---
title: "Why Is the OTP Not Received on Email for 2FA?"
---

## Problem

A one-time password (OTP) is not received through email for two-factor authentication (2FA).

## Solution

Perform the following steps:

1. Ensure that 2FA is enabled in the workspace.
2. Ensure that you have entered the correct email address.
3. Check your spam or junk folder for the OTP email.
4. Ensure that the MoEngage domain is whitelisted by the IT team.
5. Ideally, the OTP Verification page looks like this: <img alt="OTP verification.png" src="/images/moengage_b68ec2.png" />
6. If the 2-Step Verification page shows a warning message, set up a 2-step verification on your device. The warning message states: "WARNING! Your admin has enabled 2-step verification for your account. Please set it up on your device to continue access." <img alt="OTP warning.png" src="/images/moengage_fed8a4.png" />
