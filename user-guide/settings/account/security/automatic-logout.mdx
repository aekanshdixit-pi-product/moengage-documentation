---
title: "Automatic Logout"
description: "Understand when and why MoEngage automatically logs you out of your account, and how to log back in after each automatic logout scenario."
---

MoEngage automatically logs you out of your account in some scenarios. The following table lists those scenarios and how you can log in to your account again:

| Scenario | To log in again: |
| --- | --- |
| Your session expires. | <ol><li>Go to the login URL of your MoEngage account.</li><li>[Log in using your credentials](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/log-in-to-moengage).</li></ol> |
| Your Admin has not enabled 2-step verification at the workspace level, and you have logged in to your account. You or your Admin enables 2FA settings for your workspace. | <ol><li>[Set up 2-step verification on your device](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/set-up-2-step-verification-on-your-device).</li><li>Go to the login URL of your MoEngage account.</li><li>Complete 2-step verification and log in using your workspace credentials.</li></ol> |
| Your Admin enables Google Sign-In or SSO as the login method for your workspace. | <ol><li>Go to the login URL of your MoEngage account.</li><li>Complete 2-step verification.</li><li>[Log in using Google Sign-In or SSO](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/log-in-to-moengage).</li></ol> |
| Your Admin revokes your access within the workspace. | Request your Admin to restore your access. You cannot log in to that workspace until access is granted again. |
| Your workspace is blocked, archived, or removed. | Contact your Admin. If other workspaces are available to you, [select a different workspace](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/select-a-workspace) to continue working. |
| The login method configured for your workspace changes. | Go to the login URL of your MoEngage account. Complete 2-step verification and authenticate using the new login method. |
| Your password changes. | Go to the login URL of your MoEngage account. [Log in using your new password](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/log-in-to-moengage). |
| Your IT team enables firewall settings for your workspace, but your IP address does not match the list of allowed addresses. | Contact your IT team to either change your IP address or update the list of allowed IP addresses for the workspace. |
| Your Admin changes your role. | <ol><li>Go to the login URL of your MoEngage account.</li><li>[Log in using your credentials](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/log-in-to-moengage).</li></ol> |
