---
title: "Organization Details"
---

The **Organization** tab provides the details of your organization's agreement with MoEngage.

<Tip>
- You must have the **Growth** or **Enterprise** plan to view or edit your organization details.
- Users must have the **View** permission for **Usage and** **Billing** to view the organization details.
- Users must have the **Create & Manage** permission for **Usage and Billing** to edit the organization details.
</Tip>

# View Organization Details

<Info>
Users on the **Starter** plan cannot view the organization details on the **Organization** tab.  
 <img src="/images/moengage_f6aedf.png" />
</Info>

Perform the following steps to view the organization's details:

1. On the left navigation menu in the MoEngage dashboard, click **Settings** \> **Billing**.
2. On the Billing page, select the **Organization** tab.  
    <img  src="/images/moengage_6e9bac.png"  />
3. On the Organization tab, you can view the following details:

   | Section | Fields & Description |
   | --- | --- |
   | Account Details | <ul><li>**Organization name**: This field specifies the name of your organization.</li><li>**Industry**: This field specifies the industry in which your organization operates. For example, banking or e-commerce.</li><li>**Admin contacts**: This field specifies the email address(s) of your admins in the organization.<br/><br/>MoEngage will send invoices, overages, and other organization-related messages to this email address.</li></ul> |
   | Contract Information | <ul><li>**Contract Effective Date**: This field shows the date on which the contract for the organization was signed.</li><li>**Contract renewal date**: This field shows the date on which the contract is renewed for the organization.</li><li>**Billing frequency**: The field shows the frequency at which the organization is billed.</li><li>**Plan name**: This field shows the name of the current active plan configured for your workspace.<br/><br/>**Note**: To update your account, contact your MoEngage Customer Success Manager.</li><li>**FUP Value**: This field displays the threshold or limit established in the Fair Usage Policy (FUP). The FUP outlines guidelines and rules to guarantee equitable and non-discriminatory access to MoEngage resources for all customers. For more information, refer [here](/user-guide/data/key-concepts/fair-usage-policy-fup).</li><li>**FUP Type**: This field shows the type of policy (FUP) applied. The FUP type defines whether you are charged for utilising *All Events* or *Custom Events*.</li><li>**Data Retention Period**: This field shows how long your data is kept within the organization.</li><li>**Subscription code**: This field shows the subscription code entered (used to upgrade the plan details).<br/><br/>**Note**: If you have already subscribed to a plan but have not received a subscription code, contact your customer success manager.</li></ul> |

# Edit Organization Details

<Info>
You can edit only the **Admin contacts** field in the **Account details** section.
</Info>

To edit the organization details, perform the following steps:

1. On the left navigation menu in the MoEngage dashboard, click **Settings** \> **Billing**.
2. On the Billing page, select the **Organization** tab.  
    <img  src="/images/moengage_a82ab5.png"  />
3. On the **Organization** tab, click the **Edit** link.  
    <img  src="/images/moengage_51e84e.png"  />
4. On the **Edit admin contacts** pop-up window, edit the existing admin contacts as necessary. You can add multiple email addresses, separate them with a comma, and press Enter.  
    <img src="/images/moengage_cbff99.png" />
5. Click **Save**.

Your organization details are updated successfully.
