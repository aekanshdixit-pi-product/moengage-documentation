# Introducing the New MoEngage Navigation Experience

<Note>
This is an Early Access feature. To enable it for your account, contact your MoEngage Customer Success Manager (CSM) or the Support team.
</Note>

We are excited to announce a major revamp of MoEngage’s navigation, designed to make your workflow smoother, faster, and more intuitive than ever before. This complete redesign helps you find what you need more easily and accomplish tasks in significantly fewer clicks.

### Key Improvements

- **Fewer clicks, more action**: The new design significantly reduces the number of clicks required for common tasks, letting you accomplish more in less time.
- **Smarter organization**: Based on an improved information architecture, we've logically regrouped related menu items. For example, hover over Segment to find all audience-related actions consolidated in one place.
- **Enhanced discoverability**: The improved structure and cleaner visual layout make it easier to find the features you need and help you discover new capabilities to explore MoEngage’s full potential.

We are confident you will enjoy the new streamlined design. However, you can easily switch between the new and classic navigation experiences, allowing you to adapt at your own pace.

### A Quick Tour of the New Design

- **A Sleek, Collapsible Sidebar**: The new sidebar can be collapsed into a compact, icon-only bar to save screen space and allow you to focus more on your work.
- **Hover-Activated Menus**: Simply hover your mouse over a primary icon (like Analyze or Engage) to instantly open a secondary panel with all its contents, giving you access to what you need without a single click.
- **Organized menu groups**: The new menu groups related actions for a more organized workflow. For example:
  - Hover over the Dashboards menu item to find a direct shortcut to **+ New Dashboard**.
  - Hover over the Segment menu item to find all audience-related actions consolidated in one place, including direct shortcuts to **+ New Segment** and **+ New User Import**.
- **The Global "Create New" Button**: The Create New button at the top of the sidebar is your central starting point for building campaigns, flows, segments, or dashboards from anywhere in the platform.

Experience the new navigation to streamline your work.

# Generate Description for Custom Events and Attributes Using Merlin AI

Merlin AI now automatically generates descriptions for custom events and attributes within your workspace.

This feature provides clear, contextual explanations for custom attributes directly within the user interface. This assists you in accurately identifying and selecting the correct attributes during segmentation, analysis, and campaign creation.

### Key Highlights

- **Automatic Description Generation**: Merlin AI analyzes the readable name and data type of your custom attributes to generate a descriptive summary. However, this does not affect standard, system-level attributes provided by MoEngage.
- **Display on the UI**: The generated descriptions appear in the attribute selection drop-down lists in several key areas:
  - Segment filters in the Segmentation module.
  - The **Filter users** section in MoEngage Analytics.
  - The **Target audience** section during campaign creation.

For more information, refer to [Merlin AI Event and Attribute Descriptions](https://help.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/38533408220692).

# Chrome's New Spam Filter for Web Push

Google Chrome on Android now includes a spam filter that may impact the visibility of your web push notifications.

Here’s what you need to know:

- Suspicious notifications are hidden behind a "Possible spam" prompt.
- Impressions are still counted even if the message is flagged as potentially affecting CTR accuracy.
- No alerts are sent when a message is flagged.

To reduce risk:

- Use the “Test Push” feature to preview notifications on Android devices.
- Avoid using high-urgency or misleading language.
- Do not mimic system alerts.

For best practices and examples, refer to [Google Chrome Spam Filter for Web Push Notifications on Android](https://help.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/39023078633876).

# Enhancements to Email Content Blocks

MoEngage enhanced the Drag and Drop editor with the Content Blocks feature to make building reusable content faster and more intuitive.

Key Improvements:

- Introduced a dedicated tile for Content Block in the Drag and Drop editor, which allows you to add saved content effortlessly. What was once a multi-step process is now a simple drag-and-drop action, saving you time and streamlining your workflow.
- You can now save any Row from the email editor directly as an HTML Content Block. This immediately makes that asset editable, manageable, and trackable from the central Content Block library.
- Content Blocks are no longer an Enterprise-only feature. MoEngage is making them available for every single MoEngage customer. Now, every marketer can build a library of reusable, centrally-managed assets and use them not just in emails, but across Push, In-App, SMS, WhatsApp, and more.

For more information, refer to [Create Content with the Drag and Drop Editor](https://help.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/360059171931-Create-Content-with-the-Drag-and-Drop-Editor).
