# Accessibility

MoEngage has introduced new capabilities to help you create digitally accessible marketing campaigns. These features are designed to ensure your campaigns are usable by people with disabilities, aligning with best practices and upcoming regulations like the European Accessibility Act (EAA). You can create accessible campaigns across multiple channels, including Push, In-App Messaging, Email, On-Site Messaging, and Cards.

For more information, refer [here](https://help.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/20358934549140-Accessible-Campaigns-in-MoEngage).

# Campaign Audience Limit

MoEngage now supports Campaign Audience Limits, enabling marketers to control campaign reach based on real-time engagement outcomes.

<Note>
This is an Early Access feature. To enable it for your account, contact your CSM or [raise a support ticket](https://help.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/19708702327572).
</Note>

With this feature, you can:

- Define maximum limits for key campaign events such as Sent, Impressions, Opens, Delivered, and Conversions.
- Automatically pause campaigns once a desired threshold is met, ensuring smarter, outcome-driven control.
- Choose from Total Limit, Daily Limit, and Instance Limit options for flexible audience management.

Key benefits:

- Optimize ROI and prevent overspending by tying spend to actual outcomes.
- Control offer redemptions and contest entries with precision.
- Manage access to exclusive content and limited promotions.
- Optimize campaigns based on real engagement, not just volume.

For more information, refer [here](https://help.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/35579781173012-Campaign-Audience-Limit#h_01JP4SJY12YM2WAB1FYB8DSFDX).

# iOS Broadcast Live Activities

With this feature, you can:

- Display real-time updates such as order status, trip details, and live scores.
- Improve user engagement with glanceable, always-visible information.
- Customize the appearance of Live Activities to match your brand.
- Send secure, real-time updates using Live Activity tokens and MoEngage APIs.

# WhatsApp Lead Management

MoEngage now introduces WhatsApp Lead Management, a new feature that empowers you to automatically capture new leads and comprehensively track all incoming WhatsApp messages directly within MoEngage.

Key benefits include:

- Automatically generate user profiles for new contacts who initiate conversations via WhatsApp.
- Gain a complete view of all incoming customer messages, not just campaign-related replies.
- Leverage a full history of customer interactions to better understand and engage your users.

For more information, refer to [Configure WhatsApp](https://help.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/4960734358932-Configure-WhatsApp).

# Self-Service for Email Onboarding

MoEngage now introduces the Self-Service for Email Onboarding, a streamlined process for setting up the necessary infrastructure and configurations to send emails directly through the MoEngage platform. This includes crucial steps like setting up email servers and ensuring domain authentication, all designed to enhance deliverability and comply with the latest guidelines from major mailbox providers such as Google, Yahoo, and Microsoft. For more information, refer to [Self-Service for Email Onboarding](https://help.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/31225171183124-Self-Service-for-Email-Onboarding).

<Info>
This feature is accessible only if you are using the MoEngage SendGrid delivery.
</Info>
