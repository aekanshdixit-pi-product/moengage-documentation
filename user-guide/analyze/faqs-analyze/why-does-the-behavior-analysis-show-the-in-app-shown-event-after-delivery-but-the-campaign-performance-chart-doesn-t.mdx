## Problem

- The behavior analysis for an In-app Messaging campaign set to deliver on a particular day displays the In-app shown event for days after delivery. <img alt="analyze behaviour.png" src="/images/moengage_eb0ca9.png" />
- However, the Campaign Performance chart in the campaign shows only impressions on the delivery date. <img alt="campaign performance.png" src="/images/moengage_c838c0.png" />

## Explanation

This discrepancy is due to the difference in time considered for Campaign Impressions and the Analyze Module.

- Campaign Impressions are based on *Event Time*.
- Data in Analytics and Segmentation is based on *Event Received Time* (server time).

See the following image for reference: <img alt="event time ed.png" src="/images/moengage_8a8d65.png" />

You can verify this by checking the **Event Time** and **Event Received Time** on the User profile. For more information, refer to [Event Time and Received Time](https://help.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/5426529660052-Event-Time-and-Received-Time).
