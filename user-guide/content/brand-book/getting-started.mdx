## Overview

The MoEngage **Brand Book** is a centralized repository for managing brand identity assets. This repository helps you maintain a consistent visual identity across all marketing channels. When you configure standard brand logos, color palettes, typography styles, button attributes, and hyperlink decorators, the changes are automatically applied and can be used across your workspaces.

A consistent presentation improves brand recognition, reduces content creation time, and removes the need to manually enter hex codes or reconfigure text formats for every email, push notification, landing page, and in-app message campaign. After publication, your Brand Book attributes also integrate with the Merlin AI content engine to generate copy that aligns with your brand voice.

![Brand Book overview](/images/brand-book-overview.png)

<Info>
  **Scope of the current release**

  This release covers the visual identity assets: logos, colors, fonts, typography, buttons, and hyperlinks. The Communication tab for brand voice and tone integrates with Merlin AI as those capabilities expand. Only one Brand Book per workspace is supported in this release.
</Info>

## Access the Brand Book

The Brand Book is managed at the account level. To access it, perform the following steps:

1. On the sidebar menu in MoEngage, hover over the Settings icon ![Settings icon](/images/brand-book-settings-icon.png). The Settings menu expands.
2. Click **Account**, and then click **Brand book**.

<Info>
  The Brand Book is positioned as a standalone option in the navigation pane, separated from standard configuration settings.
</Info>

## Permissions and Access Control

The system enforces role-based access control for managing brand assets. Review your assigned permissions inside your current workspace to verify your access:

| Workspace Account Role        | Access Permission Level    | Functional Details                                                                                                                                                  |
| ----------------------------- | -------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Admin / Manager**           | Read, Create, and Modify   | Full access to ingest assets, modify typography ranges, upload font packages, clear assets, and publish Brand Book schemas.                                         |
| **All Other Workspace Roles** | Read-Only (View Only mode) | View published parameters and select preset assets inside campaign builders. Editing controls, upload fields, and delete icons are dimmed and unavailable.          |
