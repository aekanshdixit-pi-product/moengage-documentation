---
title: "Communication guidelines"
description: "Define brand voice, tone, and editorial personality metrics that Merlin AI reads when generating campaign copy."
keywords: ["communication guidelines", "brand voice", "brand tone", "merlin ai"]
---

Select the **Communication** tab next to the brand assets panel to define your brand voice, tone, and editorial personality metrics.

This module acts as an instruction engine for the copy platform. When campaign creators use the **Merlin AI** content engine to write copy for email subject lines, push notifications, or SMS bodies, Merlin reads these stored linguistic parameters automatically. This removes the need to manually enter tone variables during copy generation, allowing Merlin to generate copy that aligns with your brand voice.
