Signing up with MoEngage creates your first workspace. You must complete this one-time step based on your organization's requirements.

<Tip>
  **Prerequisite**

  You must have a valid subscription code from MoEngage. Contact your sales representative to obtain your subscription code.
</Tip>

# Create a Workspace

1. Visit the following URL:
   ```auto
   https://dashboard-0X.moengage.com/v4/#/auth/signup
   ```
   The `X` in the URL refers to the MoEngage data center (DC). You can find your DC number by referring to the [data center and API endpoint mapping](/user-guide/data/key-concepts/data-centers-in-moengage). The Create your account page appears. <img src="/images/moengage_cc12ed.png" />
2. Create your workspace using one of the following methods:
   - [Using a password](#using-a-password)
   - [Using your Google account](#using-your-google-account)

## Using a Password

1. In the **Company name (Workspace name)** box, type your company name or workspace name. This will be the name of your workspace in the MoEngage dashboard.
2. In the **Work email** box, type your work email.
3. In the **Password** box, type your password. Ensure the password meets the following criteria:
   - Contains 8 to 20 characters.
   - Contains one capital letter.
   - Contains one number.
   - Contains one special character from: `! @ # $ % ^ & * ( ) _ ? / , < > ; : { } | ~` with no spaces.
4. In the **Subscription code** box, type your subscription code.
5. Select the **I agree to the Terms of use and Privacy policy** check box.
6. Click **Get Started**.
   ![](/images/moengage_6e2c52.png)
   MoEngage sends a verification email to your registered email address.
   ![](/images/moengage_6a19ac.png)
   <Info>
     If you haven't received the verification email, click **Resend Verification Email**.
   </Info>
7. Open the email from MoEngage and click the activation link.
   ![](/images/moengage_022b77.png)
   After verification, you are directed to the login page.
   ![](/images/moengage_53aaf0.png)

## Using Your Google Account

1. Click **Continue with Google**. <img src="/images/moengage_47ec15.png" />
2. On the **Sign in with Google** page, choose your Google account. <img src="/images/moengage_0566b4.png" />
3. On the Sign in to moengage.com page, click **Continue**. <img src="/images/moengage_7a259b.png" /> The **Finish signing up** page appears. <img src="/images/moengage_70c13a.png" />
4. In the **Company name (Workspace name)** box, type your company name or workspace name.
5. In the **Subscription code** box, type your subscription code.
6. Click **Continue**.
   ![Continue button](/images/moengage_9b2228.png)
   Your workspace is created. You are directed to the login page.
   ![](/images/moengage_53aaf0.png)

<Info>
  MoEngage enables 2-step verification by default to protect your workspace from unauthorized access. When you log in for the first time, MoEngage prompts you to set up 2-step verification on your device. For more information, refer to [Set Up 2-Step Verification on Your Device](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/log-in-to-moengage#set-up-2-step-verification-on-your-device).
</Info>

# Finish Your Workspace Setup as an Invited User

If someone invited you to a MoEngage workspace, you must complete a one-time setup before logging in for the first time.

<Tip>
  **Prerequisites**

  An Admin or Manager must have invited you to your company's MoEngage workspace. You will receive a welcome email from **Moengage Alerts** ([alerts@moengage.com](mailto:alerts@moengage.com)) with the following subject line:

  `<Name_of_the_inviter>` has invited you to join MoEngage for `<Name_of_the_workspace>`

  ![Invitation email](/images/moengage_496218.png)

  If you haven't received this email, contact your company's MoEngage Admin or Manager. Roles with the **Create & Manage** permission under the **Team Members** component can send invitations. For more information, refer to [Invite a Member](/user-guide/settings/account/team-management/manage-members).
</Tip>

1. Open the email you received from **Moengage Alerts**.
2. Click the **click here** link. <img src="/images/moengage_431df0.png" alt="Click here link in invitation email" /> You are taken to the Finish account setup page. <img src="/images/moengage_a63a90.png" alt="Finish account setup page" />
3. Complete your workspace setup using one of the following methods:
   - [Set up a password](#set-up-a-password)
   - [Sign in with Google](#sign-in-with-google)

## Set Up a Password

1. On the Finish account setup page, your email address is automatically populated in the **Email** box. <img src="/images/moengage_a63a90.png" alt="Email pre-filled on setup page" />
2. In the **Password** box, enter a new password. Ensure the password meets the following criteria:
   - Contains 8 to 20 characters.
   - Contains one capital letter.
   - Contains one number.
   - Contains one special character from: `! @ # $ % ^ & * ( ) _ ? / , < > ; : { } | ~` with no spaces.
3. Click the **Terms of use** link and read the terms.
4. Click the **Privacy policy** link and read the policy.
5. Select the **I agree to the Terms of use and Privacy policy** check box.
6. Click **Set Password and Login**. Your workspace setup is complete. You are directed to the Login to your accounts page. <img src="/images/moengage_703e7a.png" alt="Login to your accounts page" />
   <Info>
     Bookmark your MoEngage login page for faster future access. If you forget your login URL, you can find it on the [MoEngage global login page](https://dashboard.moengage.com/). For more information, refer to [Find Your Login Page](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/log-in-to-moengage#find-your-login-page).
   </Info>
7. [Log in to your workspace](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/log-in-to-moengage).

## Sign In with Google

1. On the Finish account setup page, click **Continue with Google**. <img src="/images/moengage_b08040.png" alt="Continue with Google button" />
2. On the Choose an account page, choose your Google account. <img src="/images/moengage_42f67e.png" alt="Choose an account page" />
3. On the You're signing back in to moengage.com page, click **Continue**. <img src="/images/moengage_bdb216.png" alt="Sign in confirmation page" /> Your workspace setup is complete. You are directed to the Login to your accounts page. <img src="/images/moengage_703e7a.png" alt="Login to your accounts page" />
   <Info>
     Bookmark your MoEngage login page for faster future access. If you forget your login URL, you can find it on the [MoEngage global login page](https://dashboard.moengage.com/). For more information, refer to [Find Your Login Page](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/log-in-to-moengage#find-your-login-page).
   </Info>
4. [Log in to your workspace](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/log-in-to-moengage).
