Signing up with MoEngage is the essential first step to unlocking MoEngage's powerful features. You must create a new account in MoEngage (a one-time task) based on your organization's requirements.

<Tip>
You must have a valid subscription code from MoEngage. Contact your sales representative at MoEngage to obtain your subscription code.
</Tip>

# Create a New Account

Perform the following steps to create a new account in MoEngage:

1. Visit the following URL:

   ```auto
   https://dashboard-0X.moengage.com/v4/#/auth/signup
   ```

   The 'X' in the URL refers to the MoEngage data center (DC). MoEngage hosts each customer in a different DC. You can find your DC number (value of X) and replace the value of 'X' in the URL by referring to the DC and API endpoint mapping [here](/user-guide/data/key-concepts/data-centers-in-moengage). The Create your account page appears.  
    <img  src="/images/moengage_cc12ed.png"  />
2. Complete your account creation using one of the following methods:
   - [Using a password](#h_01JG6F6BB77VWQQEKJ1MG51SKR)
   - [Using your Google account](#h_01HCF71DEQY09CG9KXW1AYQ73F)

## Using a Password

Perform the following steps to create a new account using a password:

1. In the **Company name (Workspace name)** box, type your company name or workspace name. This will be the name of your account in the MoEngage dashboard, which is called a *workspace*.
2. In the **Work email**box, type your work email.
3. In the **Password** box, type your password. Ensure the password meets the following criteria:  
   - Contains 8 to 20 characters.
   - Contains one capital letter.
   - Contains one number.
   - Contains one special character among !@#$%^&\*()\_?/,`<>`;:{}|~ and no space.
4. In the **Subscription code** box, type your subscription code.
5. Select the **I agree to the Terms of use and Privacy policy** check box.
6. Click **Get Started**.  
    <img  src="/images/moengage_6e2c52.png"  />   
   You are directed to the following page. MoEngage sends an email to your registered email address for verification.  
    <img  src="/images/moengage_6a19ac.png"  />

   <Info>
   If you have not received an email to activate your account, click **Resend Verification Email**.
   </Info>
7. Open the email you received from MoEngage and click the linkto activate your account.  
    **<img src="/images/moengage_022b77.png" />** After the verification, you are directed to the login page of the MoEngage dashboard.  
    <img  src="/images/moengage_53aaf0.png"  />

   <Info>
   MoEngage enables 2-step verification by default to protect your account from unauthorized access using an authenticator app. When you try to log in, MoEngage prompts you to set up 2-step verification. You must configure a one-time verification setup on your device to proceed with login. For more information, refer to [Set Up 2-Step Verification on Your Device](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/set-up-2-step-verification-on-your-device).
   </Info>

## Using Your Google Account

Perform the following steps to create a new account using your Google account:

1. Click **Continue with Google**.  
    <img  src="/images/moengage_47ec15.png"  />
2. On the **Sign in with Google** page, choose your Google account.  
    <img  src="/images/moengage_0566b4.png"  />
3. On the Sign in to moengage.com page, click **Continue**.  
    <img  src="/images/moengage_7a259b.png"  />   
   The **Finish signing up** page appears.  
    <img  src="/images/moengage_70c13a.png"  />
4. In the **Company name (Workspace name)** box, type your company name or workspace name. This will be the name of your account in the MoEngage dashboard, which is called a *workspace*.
5. In the **Subscription code** box, type your subscription code.
6. Click **Continue**.  
    <img alt="4545.png"  src="/images/moengage_9b2228.png"  />   
   You have now successfully created an account in MoEngage. You are directed to the login page of the MoEngage dashboard.  
    <img  src="/images/moengage_53aaf0.png"  />

<Info>
MoEngage enables 2-step verification by default to protect your account from unauthorized access using an authenticator app. When you try to log in, MoEngage prompts you to set up 2-step verification. You must configure a one-time verification setup on your device to proceed with login. For more information, refer to [Set Up 2-Step Verification on Your Device](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/set-up-2-step-verification-on-your-device).
</Info>
