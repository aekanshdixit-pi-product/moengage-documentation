title: "Forgot Password?"
---

If you forget the password for your MoEngage account, you can create a new password anytime.

## Reset Your Password

To reset your password, perform the following steps:

1. Go to your MoEngage account login page.
2. Click **Forgot password**.  
    <img  src="/images/moengage_d26a75.png"  />   
   The Forgot password page appears.
3. In the **Email** box, type your MoEngage email ID.
4. Click **Send Link**.  
    <img src="/images/moengage_74e4cd.png" />   
   You receive an email from MoEngage with a link to reset your password.
5. Open the email you received from MoEngage and click the link to reset your password. **<img  src="/images/moengage_d301e8.png"  />**
6. On the **Reset** p**assword** page, enter the following details:
   1. In the **New password** box, type your new password. Ensure the password meets the following criteria:  
      - Contains 8 to 20 characters.
      - Contains one capital letter.
      - Contains one number.
      - Contains one special character among !@#$%^&\*()\_?/,\<\>;:{}|~ and no space.
   2. In the **Confirmed password** box, type the same password for confirmation.  
      <Info>
Choose a password that you haven't already used.
</Info>
   3. Click **Reset Password**.  
       <img src="/images/moengage_8b2aef.png" />   
      You have successfully reset your password, and MoEngage will redirect you to the login page. You can [log in to your MoEngage dashboard](https://help.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/34852261258004) using the new password.
