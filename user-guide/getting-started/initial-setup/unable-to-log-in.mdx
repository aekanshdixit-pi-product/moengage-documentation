---

Find your situation below and follow the steps to regain access.

- [Forgot your password](#forgot-your-password)
- [Lost access to your 2-step verification app](#lost-access-to-your-2-step-verification-app)
- [Use a recovery code](#use-a-recovery-code)

## Forgot your password

1. Go to your MoEngage login page.
2. Click **Forgot password**.
   <img src="/images/moengage_d26a75.png" />
   The Forgot password page appears.
3. In the **Email** box, type your email address.
4. Click **Send Link**.
   <img src="/images/moengage_74e4cd.png" />
   You receive an email from MoEngage with a password reset link.
5. Open the email and click the link.
   <img src="/images/moengage_d301e8.png" />
6. On the **Reset password** page:
   1. In the **New password** box, type your new password. Ensure it meets the following criteria:
      - Contains 8 to 20 characters.
      - Contains one capital letter.
      - Contains one number.
      - Contains one special character among !@#\$%^&\*()\_?/,`<>`;:`{}`|~ and no space.
   2. In the **Confirmed password** box, retype the password.
      <Info>
        Choose a password you haven't used before.
      </Info>
   3. Click **Reset Password**.
      <img src="/images/moengage_8b2aef.png" />

You are redirected to the login page. [Log in to MoEngage](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/log-in-to-moengage) using your new password.

## Lost access to your 2-step verification app

Use this if your authenticator app isn't generating codes, your device is lost, or you've replaced your phone. MoEngage sends a reset link to your registered email address.

1. [Start the login process](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/log-in-to-moengage) using your usual login method.
2. On the 2-Step Verification page, click **Receive reset 2-step verification link via email**.
   <img src="/images/moengage_d1089b.png" />
3. Click **Send link**.
   <img src="/images/moengage_60b241.png" />
   A reset link is sent to your registered email address.
4. Open the email from MoEngage and click the link to reset your 2FA credentials.
   <img src="/images/moengage_b61f2f.png" />
   You are taken to the Login to your accounts page in a new browser tab.
   <img src="/images/moengage_529598.png" />
5. Log in using your credentials. You are taken to the 2-Step Verification page.
6. Click **Setup your device** and set up 2-step verification on your new device. For the full setup steps, refer to [Set Up 2-Step Verification on Your Device](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/set-up-2-step-verification-on-your-device).
   <img src="/images/moengage_2a04b3.png" />

## Use a recovery code

Use this if you can't access your authenticator app but have your downloaded recovery codes.

<Tip>
  **Prerequisite**

  You must have previously downloaded your recovery codes. Recovery codes are provided when you [set up 2-step verification](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/set-up-2-step-verification-on-your-device). Each code can only be used once, and MoEngage provides 10 codes per setup.
</Tip>

1. [Start the login process](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/log-in-to-moengage) using your usual login method.
2. On the 2-Step Verification page, click **Enter a 2-step recovery code**.
   ![2-step verification page](/images/moengage_afb7a9.png)
3. In the **Recovery code** box, type one of your recovery codes.
   <Info>
     Each recovery code is single-use. After use, it is marked invalid and cannot be used again.
   </Info>
4. Click **Verify**.
   ![Recovery code entry](/images/moengage_436916.png)
   You are logged in to the MoEngage dashboard.

After logging in with a recovery code, set up 2-step verification on a new or restored device to ensure future access. For more information, refer to [Lost access to your 2-step verification app](#lost-access-to-your-2-step-verification-app).
