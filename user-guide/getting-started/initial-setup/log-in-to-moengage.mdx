This page explains how to log in to your MoEngage account.

MoEngage login uses two stages of authentication:

1. **User authentication** — Verify your identity using 2-step verification (2FA). This step is the same regardless of which workspace you are logging in to.
2. **Workspace authentication** — Authenticate into a specific workspace using the login method your Admin configured: password, Google Sign-In, or SSO.

After user authentication, you can log in to your last used workspace or choose a different one from a list of all workspaces you have access to.

<Tip>
  **Prerequisites**
  - **First login**: Complete your one-time account setup before logging in for the first time. For more information, refer to [Finish Your Account Setup as an Invited User](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/finish-your-account-setup-as-an-invited-user).
  - **Subsequent logins**: Go to the login page for your data center. If you do not know the URL, find it at [dashboard.moengage.com](https://dashboard.moengage.com) using your email address. For more information, refer to [Navigate to Your Data Center's Login Page](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/navigate-to-your-accounts-login-page).
  - **2-step verification**: Set up 2-step verification on your device before your first login. For more information, refer to [Set Up 2-Step Verification on Your Device](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/set-up-2-step-verification-on-your-device).
</Tip>

# Log In to Your Last Used Workspace

Use this flow to log in directly to the workspace you last accessed.

## Step 1: Verify Your Identity

1. In a web browser, go to the login page for your data center.
2. In the **Work email** box, type your email address and click **Continue**.
3. On the 2-Step Verification page, open your authenticator app and find the current 6-digit code for your MoEngage account.
4. Type the code in the **Verification code** boxes and click **Verify**.

<Info>
  If you have not set up 2-step verification yet, you are prompted to configure it before continuing. For more information, refer to [Set Up 2-Step Verification on Your Device](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/set-up-2-step-verification-on-your-device).
</Info>

## Step 2: Authenticate to Your Workspace

After verifying your identity, complete workspace authentication. The steps depend on the login method your Admin has configured. For more information, refer to [Set Up the Login Method](/user-guide/settings/account/security/set-up-the-login-method).

### Using a Password

<Info>
  If your Admin configured the login method as **Password or Google Sign-In**, you can log in using either a password or the **Sign in with Google** option.
</Info>

1. In the **Password** box, type your password.
2. Click **Login**. You are logged in to the MoEngage dashboard.

### Using Google Sign-In

<Info>
  If your Admin configured the login method as **Google Sign-In only**, you must use this option. You cannot log in with a password.
</Info>

1. Click **Sign in with Google**.
2. On the **Choose an account** page, select your Google account.
3. Click **Continue**. You are logged in to the MoEngage dashboard.

### Using SSO

<Info>
  If your Admin configured the login method as **Single Sign-On (SSO) only**, you must use SSO. You cannot log in with a password or Google Sign-In.
</Info>

1. Click **Login using SSO**. You are redirected to your organization's Identity Provider (IdP).
2. Authenticate using your IdP credentials.
3. In the IdP dashboard, click the MoEngage app. You are logged in to the MoEngage dashboard.

<Info>
  For IdP-initiated SSO: if 2-step verification is enabled for your workspace, you are prompted to complete 2FA before accessing the dashboard.
</Info>

# Select a Different Workspace at Login

After completing user authentication (Step 1 above), you can select any workspace you have access to instead of logging in to your last used workspace. This is useful when you need to access a workspace other than your most recent one.

For more information, refer to [Select a Workspace](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/select-a-workspace).
