<Tip>
  **Prerequisites**

  - **First login (as an invited user)**: Complete your one-time account setup before logging in. For more information, refer to [Finish Your Account Setup as an Invited User](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/finish-your-account-setup-as-an-invited-user).
  - **2-step verification**: If 2-step verification is enabled for your account, set it up on your device during your first login. For more information, refer to [Set Up 2-Step Verification on Your Device](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/set-up-2-step-verification-on-your-device).
</Tip>

## Find Your Login URL

MoEngage hosts each workspace in a specific data center, so your login URL includes a data center number — for example, `dashboard-01.moengage.com`. If you don't know your URL or didn't bookmark it:

1. Go to [dashboard.moengage.com](https://dashboard.moengage.com/v4/#/auth).
   <img alt="MoEngage single login page" src="/images/moengage_d01571.png" />
2. In the **Work email** box, type your email address.
3. Click **Continue**. You are redirected to your data center's login page.
   <img alt="Data center login page" src="/images/moengage_a879ea.png" />
   <Info>
     Your email address is pre-filled on the login page so you can confirm it's correct. If it isn't you, click **Not you?** to return to the single login page.

     If you belong to multiple MoEngage accounts, you are redirected to the login page of your last used account. To access a different account, go to its URL directly.
   </Info>

Bookmark your login URL after you land on it.

## Login Methods

- [Using a password](#using-a-password)
- [Using Sign in with Google](#using-sign-in-with-google)
- [Using SSO](#using-sso)

## Using a Password

<Info>
  Available when your Admin has configured the login method as **Password or Google sign in**. For more information, refer to [Set Up the Login Method](/user-guide/settings/account/security/set-up-the-login-method).
</Info>

1. Go to your MoEngage login URL. The Login to your accounts page appears.
   ![Login page](/images/moengage_155710.png)
2. In the **Work email** box, type your email address.
3. In the **Password** box, type your password.
4. Click **Login**.

If 2-step verification is enabled for your account, you are prompted to verify. See [Complete 2-step verification](#complete-2-step-verification).

## Using Sign in with Google

<Info>
  Available when your Admin has configured the login method as **Password or Google sign in** or **Google Sign In only**. For more information, refer to [Set Up the Login Method](/user-guide/settings/account/security/set-up-the-login-method).
</Info>

1. Go to your MoEngage login URL. The Login to your accounts page appears.
   ![Login page](/images/moengage_155710.png)
2. Click **Sign in with Google**.
3. On the **Choose an account** page, choose your Google account.
   ![Choose a Google account](/images/moengage_889824.png)
4. On the **Sign in to moengage.com** page, click **Continue**.
   ![Sign in to moengage.com](/images/moengage_c8a1fa.png)

If 2-step verification is enabled for your account, you are prompted to verify. See [Complete 2-step verification](#complete-2-step-verification).

## Using SSO

<Info>
  Available when your Admin has configured the login method as **Single Sign On (SSO) only**. You cannot log in using a password or Google in this mode. For more information, refer to [Set Up the Login Method](/user-guide/settings/account/security/set-up-the-login-method).
</Info>

1. Go to your MoEngage login URL. The Login to your accounts page appears.
2. Click **Login using SSO**.
   ![Login using SSO](/images/moengage_d00957.png)
   The Login with Single Sign On page appears.
3. In the **Work Email** box, type your email address.
4. Click **Login**.
   ![SSO login page](/images/moengage_205889.png)
   You are redirected to your organization's identity provider (IdP) for authentication.
5. Log in to the IdP using your credentials.
6. In the IdP dashboard, click the MoEngage app.

If 2-step verification is enabled for your account, you are prompted to verify. See [Complete 2-step verification](#complete-2-step-verification).

## Complete 2-step verification

After you log in, if 2-step verification is enabled for your account, the 2-Step Verification page appears.

- **If you have not configured 2-step verification on your device yet**: You are prompted to set it up. For more information, refer to [Set Up 2-Step Verification on Your Device](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/set-up-2-step-verification-on-your-device).
  ![2-step verification setup prompt](/images/moengage_943be8.png)

- **If you have already set up 2-step verification**:
  ![2-step verification page](/images/moengage_7fbf4b.png)
  1. Open the authenticator app on your device and find the 6-digit code for your MoEngage account.
  2. In the **Verification code** boxes, type the code.
  3. Click **Verify**. You are logged in to the MoEngage dashboard.

  <Info>
    If you can't access your authenticator app or have run out of recovery codes, refer to [Unable to Log In](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/unable-to-log-in).
  </Info>
