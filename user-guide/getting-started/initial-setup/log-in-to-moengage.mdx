  - **First login (as an invited user)**: You must complete a one-time workspace setup before logging in for the first time. </Tip>

## Find Your Login Page

MoEngage hosts each workspace in a specific data center. Your login URL includes the data center identifier — for example, `dashboard-01.moengage.com` for DC-01. If you don't know your login URL, use the MoEngage global login page to find it:

1. Go to [dashboard.moengage.com](https://dashboard.moengage.com/v4/#/auth).
   <img alt="MoEngage global login page" src="/images/moengage_d01571.png" />
2. In the **Work email** box, type your email address.
3. Click **Continue**. MoEngage redirects you to your workspace's login page on the correct data center.
   <img alt="Data center login page" src="/images/moengage_a879ea.png" />
   <Info>
     Your email address is pre-filled on the login page for verification. If it's not your email, click **Not you?** to return to the global login page.
   </Info>
   <Info>
     If Single Sign-On (SSO) is enabled on your last used workspace, you are automatically redirected to the Login with Single Sign-On page.
     <img alt="SSO redirect page" src="/images/moengage_ad225e.png" />
   </Info>

Bookmark your login URL for faster access in the future. For more information on data centers, refer to [Data Centers in MoEngage](/user-guide/data/key-concepts/data-centers-in-moengage).

## Log In Methods

You can log in to your MoEngage workspace using the following methods:

- [Using a password](#using-a-password)
- [Using the Sign in with Google option](#using-the-sign-in-with-google-option)
- [Using your company's SSO provider](#using-sso)

## Using a Password

<Info>
  If your MoEngage Admin set up the login method as **Password or Google sign in**, you can log in using either your email and password or the **Sign in with Google** button. For more information, refer to [Set Up the Login Method](/user-guide/settings/account/security/set-up-the-login-method).
</Info>

1. In a web browser, go to the URL of your MoEngage workspace. The Login to your accounts page appears.
   ![Login page](/images/moengage_155710.png)
2. In the **Work email** box, type your email address.
3. In the **Password** box, type your password.
4. Click **Login**.
5. If 2-step verification is enabled for your workspace, see [Complete 2-Step Verification](#complete-2-step-verification) below.

## Using the Sign in with Google Option

<Info>
  If your MoEngage Admin set up the login method as **Google Sign In only**, you must use the **Sign in with Google** option. You cannot log in using a password. For more information, refer to [Set Up the Login Method](/user-guide/settings/account/security/set-up-the-login-method).
</Info>

1. In a web browser, go to the URL of your MoEngage workspace. The Login to your accounts page appears.
   ![Login page](/images/moengage_155710.png)
2. Click **Sign in with Google**.
3. On the **Choose an account** page, choose your Google account.
   ![Choose a Google account](/images/moengage_889824.png)
4. On the **Sign in to moengage.com** page, click **Continue**.
   ![Sign in to moengage.com](/images/moengage_c8a1fa.png)
5. If 2-step verification is enabled for your workspace, see [Complete 2-Step Verification](#complete-2-step-verification) below.

## Using SSO

<Info>
  If your MoEngage Admin set up the login method as **Single Sign On (SSO) only**, you must log in using SSO. You cannot use a password or the **Sign in with Google** option. For more information, refer to [Set Up the Login Method](/user-guide/settings/account/security/set-up-the-login-method).
</Info>

1. In a web browser, go to the URL of your MoEngage workspace. The Login to your accounts page appears.
2. Click **Login using SSO**.
   ![Login using SSO](/images/moengage_d00957.png)
   The Login with Single Sign On page appears.
3. In the **Work Email** box, type your email address.
4. Click **Login**.
   ![SSO login page](/images/moengage_205889.png)
   You are redirected to your organization's Identity Provider (IdP) for authentication.
5. Log in to the SSO IdP using your credentials.
6. In the SSO IdP dashboard, click the MoEngage app.
7. If 2-step verification is enabled for your workspace, see [Complete 2-Step Verification](#complete-2-step-verification) below.

## Complete 2-Step Verification

If 2-step verification is enabled, MoEngage shows the 2-Step Verification page after you authenticate.

- **If you haven't configured 2-step verification on your device yet**, the page prompts you to set it up. For more information, refer to [Set Up 2-Step Verification on Your Device](#set-up-2-step-verification-on-your-device).
  ![2-step verification setup prompt](/images/moengage_943be8.png)
- **If you've already configured 2-step verification**, enter the code from your authenticator app:
  ![2-step verification code entry](/images/moengage_7fbf4b.png)
  1. Open the authenticator app on your device and find the 6-digit code for your MoEngage workspace.
  2. On the 2-Step Verification page, type the code in the **Verification code** boxes.
  3. Click **Verify**. You are logged in to the MoEngage dashboard.

<Tip>
  If you cannot complete 2-step verification — for example, you've forgotten your password, lost your device, or run out of recovery codes — see [Unable to Log In](#unable-to-log-in).
</Tip>

## Set Up 2-Step Verification on Your Device

MoEngage enables 2-step verification by default to protect your workspace from unauthorized access. When 2-step verification is required, you must complete a one-time setup on your device using an authenticator app. After setup, your authenticator app generates a 6-digit code you'll use each time you log in.

<Tip>
  **Prerequisites**

  You must have an authenticator app on your phone. MoEngage supports apps such as Google Authenticator and Twilio Authy.

  - Google Authenticator: [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.authenticator2&hl=en_IN&gl=US) | [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/google-authenticator/id388497605)
  - Twilio Authy: [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.authy.authy) | [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/twilio-authy/id494168017)
</Tip>

### When You're Prompted to Set Up 2-Step Verification

You'll be prompted to set up 2-step verification in these scenarios:

- Your admin signs up for MoEngage or creates a new workspace. MoEngage enables 2-step verification by default.
- You're invited to a workspace that has 2-step verification enabled. After [finishing your workspace setup](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/sign-up-with-moengage-or-create-a-new-account-in-moengage#finish-your-workspace-setup-as-an-invited-user), MoEngage prompts you to set up 2-step verification.
- Your admin enables 2-step verification after you've already logged in, and you're automatically logged out. You're prompted on your next login.
- You enable 2-step verification for your own workspace in **My Profile**.

In each scenario you land on the 2-Step Verification page:

![2-Step Verification page](/images/moengage_3a7ace.png)

### Configure 2-Step Verification

1. On the 2-Step Verification page, click **Setup your device**.
   <img src="/images/moengage_3a7ace.png" alt="Setup your device button" />
   The Set your device authentication page appears.
   <img src="/images/moengage_8c9228.png" alt="Set your device authentication page" />

   />
3. Use one of the following options to add MoEngage to your authenticator app:
   - **Using a QR code**
     1. Tap **Scan a QR code**.

          style={{ width:"35%" }}

        />
     2. Scan the QR code shown on the **Set your device authentication** page. Your authenticator app adds MoEngage and begins generating 6-digit codes.
   - **Using a setup key**
     1. Tap **Enter a setup key**.
        <img
          src="/images/moengage_e6d745.png"
          style={{ width:"36%" }}
          alt="Enter a setup key option"
        />
     2. On the **Enter account details** screen, fill in the following fields:
        | Field | Required | Description |
        | --- | --- | --- |
        | Account name | Optional | Type your account name. |
        | Your key | Yes | Type the 32-digit secret key shown on the **Set your device authentication** page. <br /><img alt="Setup key" src="/images/moengage_331114.png" /> |
        | Type of key | Optional | **Time based** (OTP from current time) or **Counter based** (OTP from incrementing counter). |
        | <img src="/images/moengage_c59fc2.png" alt="Key type selection" /> |  |  |

### Troubleshoot 2-Step Verification Issues

If you're having trouble with 2-step verification:

- Make sure both your login device and the device with your authenticator app are set to the same timezone. Codes are time-based and expire after 30 seconds.
- Clear your browser's cookies and cache.
- Verify you're using the correct login URL for your workspace. Each workspace is hosted on a specific data center. For more information, refer to [Data Centers in MoEngage](/user-guide/data/key-concepts/data-centers-in-moengage).
- If you don't receive the reset email, check that your organization hasn't blocked the MoEngage domain.

If the issue persists, contact the MoEngage Support team or your Customer Success Manager (CSM).

## Recovery Codes

Recovery codes are a set of 10 one-time-use backup codes that let you log in to your MoEngage workspace when your primary 2-step verification device is unavailable. MoEngage generates these codes when you first set up or reset 2-step verification.

Store your recovery codes securely and do not share them. Each code can only be used once — once used, MoEngage marks it as invalid.

If you use all of your recovery codes, [reset 2-step verification](#reset-2-step-verification) to generate a new set.

## Unable to Log In

### Forgot Your Password

If you forget your password, you can reset it from the login page.

1. Go to your MoEngage workspace login page.
2. Click **Forgot password**.
   <img src="/images/moengage_d26a75.png" alt="Forgot password link" />
   The Forgot password page appears.
3. In the **Email** box, type your MoEngage email address.
4. Click **Send Link**.
   <img src="/images/moengage_74e4cd.png" alt="Send reset link" />
   MoEngage sends a password reset link to your email.
5. Open the email from MoEngage and click the reset link.
   <img src="/images/moengage_d301e8.png" alt="Password reset email" />
6. On the **Reset password** page:
   1. In the **New password** box, type your new password. The password must:
      - Contain 8 to 20 characters.
      - Contain at least one uppercase letter.
      - Contain at least one number.
      - Contain at least one special character from: `! @ # $ % ^ & * ( ) _ ? / , < > ; : { } | ~` with no spaces.
   2. In the **Confirm password** box, retype the same password.
      <Info>
        Choose a password you haven't used before.
      </Info>
   3. Click **Reset Password**.
      <img src="/images/moengage_8b2aef.png" alt="Reset password form" />

MoEngage resets your password and redirects you to the login page. [Log in](#log-in-methods) using your new password.

### Lost or Replaced Your 2-Step Verification Device

If you no longer have access to the device with your authenticator app, [reset 2-step verification](#reset-2-step-verification) to reconfigure it on a new device.

### Log In Using a Recovery Code

If your primary 2-step verification device is temporarily unavailable, use one of your downloaded recovery codes to log in.

<Tip>
  **Prerequisites**

  You must have already downloaded your recovery codes. Recovery codes are available when you first set up or reset 2-step verification. For more information, refer to [Recovery Codes](#recovery-codes).
</Tip>

1. [Log in to your workspace](#log-in-methods) using your credentials.
2. On the 2-Step Verification page, click **Enter a 2-step recovery code**.
   ![Enter a 2-step recovery code link](/images/moengage_afb7a9.png)
   The 2-Step Verification page refreshes.
3. In the **Recovery code** box, type one of your saved recovery codes.
   <Info>
     Each recovery code is unique and can only be used once. Once used, MoEngage marks it as invalid.
   </Info>
4. Click **Verify**.
   ![Verify recovery code](/images/moengage_436916.png)
   You are logged in to the MoEngage dashboard.

<Tip>
  If you've used all of your recovery codes, [reset 2-step verification](#reset-2-step-verification) to generate a new set.
</Tip>
