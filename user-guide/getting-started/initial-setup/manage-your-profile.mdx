MoEngage allows you to manage your profile for each workspace in the MoEngage dashboard. The **My Profile** section has the following tabs:

- [General](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/manage-your-profile#general)
- [Security](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/manage-your-profile#security)
- [Preferences](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/manage-your-profile#preferences)

# General

Under the **General** tab, you can edit your personal information for the current workspace.

1. In the upper-right corner, click your profile icon and then click **My Profile**. <img src="/images/moengage_4abf33.png" alt="My Profile menu" />
2. On the **My profile** page, the **General** tab is selected by default.
3. Click **Edit** in the upper-right corner. <img src="/images/moengage_25c22a.png" alt="Edit button" />
4. Add your profile picture:
   1. Hover over your profile icon and click the edit icon. <img src="/images/moengage_0ca52c.png" />
   2. Click **Update**.
   3. Select your profile picture and click **Open**.
      <Info>
        MoEngage supports only .jpeg, .jpg, and .png files.
      </Info>
5. Edit the following details:
   | Field | Description |
   | --- | --- |
   | Name | Enter your name. |
   | Email Id | This field is read-only. |
   | Role | This field is read-only. |
   | Active Since | This field is read-only. |
   | Job title | Enter your job title. |
   | Department | Enter your department name. |
   | Phone number | Select your country code and enter your phone number. |
   | Date of birth | Select your date of birth using the date picker. |
6. Click **Save**. Your personal information is updated.

# Security

The **Security** tab provides tools for enhancing security for the current workspace.

1. In the upper-right corner, click your profile icon and then click **My Profile**. <img alt="My Profile menu" src="/images/moengage_4abf33.png" />
2. On the **My profile** page, click **Security**. <img src="/images/moengage_1fde0f.png" />
3. You can perform the following tasks:
   - [Change your password](#change-your-password)
   - [Manage 2-step verification](#manage-2-step-verification)
   - [Log out of your active devices](#log-out-of-your-active-devices)

## Change Your Password

1. On the **Security** tab, click **Change password** in the upper-right corner of the **Password** section. <img src="/images/moengage_48bf1e.png" alt="Change password button" />
2. Enter the following details:
   | Field | Description |
   | --- | --- |
   | Old password | Enter the old or existing password. |
   | New password | Enter the new password. Ensure it meets the following criteria: <ul><li>Contains 8 to 20 characters.</li><li>Contains one capital letter.</li><li>Contains one number.</li><li>Contains one special character among !@#\$%^&\*()\_?/,`<>`;:`{}` ~ and no space.</li></ul> |
   | Retype new password | Retype the new password. |
3. Click **Save**. Your password is updated.

## Manage 2-Step Verification

MoEngage allows you to secure your account with 2-step verification. Your Admin can enforce it at the workspace level — for more information, refer to [2-Step Verification or Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)](/user-guide/settings/account/security/2-step-verification-or-two-factor-authentication-2fa). You can also manage it at the user level from your profile.

### Set Up 2-Step Verification for Your Workspace

<Info>
  The **Setup device** option appears when both of the following criteria are met:

  - Your Admin has disabled 2-step verification at the account (workspace) level.
  - You log in to your account for the first time.
</Info>

1. On the My profile page, click **Security**. <img src="/images/moengage_9e14ca.png" />
2. Click **Setup device** adjacent to **2-step verification**. <img src="/images/moengage_c76b87.png" />
   The Set up your device authentication pop-up window appears. <img src="/images/moengage_58eb44.png" alt="Set up your device authentication" />
3. Add MoEngage to your authenticator app by scanning the QR code or entering the setup key shown in the pop-up window, then enter the 6-digit code it generates and click **Submit**. For detailed steps, refer to [Set Up 2-Step Verification on Your Device](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/set-up-2-step-verification-on-your-device#configure-2-step-verification-on-your-device).

### Enable 2-Step Verification for Your Workspace

<Info>
  The **Enable device** option appears only if your Admin has disabled 2FA at the account (workspace) level and you have not enabled it for your workspace.
</Info>

1. On the **My profile** page, click **Security**. <img src="/images/moengage_9e14ca.png" />
2. Click the ellipse icon adjacent to **2 step verification** and then click **Enable device**. <img src="/images/moengage_1379cd.png" />
   The Enable device pop-up window appears.
3. Click **Enable**. <img src="/images/moengage_a0e357.png" />
   2FA is now enabled for your workspace.

After enabling 2FA, you are automatically logged out. When you log in again, you are prompted to set up 2-step verification on your device. For more information, refer to [Set Up 2-Step Verification on Your Device](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/set-up-2-step-verification-on-your-device).

### Remove 2-Step Verification for the Workspace

<Info>
  The **Remove device** option is visible only when both of the following criteria are met:

  - You have enabled 2FA for your workspace.
  - Your Admin disables it at the account (workspace) level.
</Info>

1. On the **My profile** page, click **Security**. <img src="/images/moengage_9e14ca.png" />
2. Click the ellipse icon adjacent to **2 step verification** and then click **Remove device**. <img src="/images/moengage_f7608e.png" />
   The Remove device pop-up window appears.
3. Click **Remove**. <img src="/images/moengage_d964c4.png" />
   2FA is now removed from your workspace.

### Change the 2-Step Verification Device for the Workspace

Use this when you get a new phone or need to move 2FA to a different device.

<Info>
  The **Change device** option is visible when you have enabled or disabled 2FA for your workspace.
</Info>

1. On the **My profile** page, click **Security**. <img src="/images/moengage_9e14ca.png" />
2. Click the ellipse icon adjacent to **2 step verification** and then click **Change device**. <img src="/images/moengage_e62b76.png" />
3. The Change device pop-up window appears. <img src="/images/moengage_b4b4c1.png" alt="Change device pop-up" />
4. Enter the 6-digit code generated by your current device's authenticator app.
5. Click **Next**.
   The **Set up your device authentication** pop-up window appears. <img src="/images/moengage_58eb44.png" alt="Set up your device authentication" />
6. Add MoEngage to your authenticator app on the new device by scanning the QR code or entering the setup key shown in the pop-up window, then enter the 6-digit code it generates and click **Submit**. For detailed steps, refer to [Set Up 2-Step Verification on Your Device](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/set-up-2-step-verification-on-your-device#configure-2-step-verification-on-your-device).
   <Info>
     After adding MoEngage to your new device, your authenticator app shows two entries — one from the old device and one from the new. Use the verification code from the new entry only.
   </Info>

## Log Out of Your Active Devices

Under **Active devices**, you can see all the devices you have used to access this workspace.

1. Click the logout icon adjacent to the device you want to log out of. <img src="/images/moengage_23de27.png" alt="Logout icon" />
2. To log out of all devices at once, click the ellipsis icon and then click **Logout of all devices**. <img src="/images/moengage_19a4f9.png" alt="Logout of all devices" />
   The Logout pop-up window appears.
3. Click **Logout**. <img src="/images/moengage_db7bcc.png" />

# Preferences

Under the **Preferences** tab, you can select your preferred language for the MoEngage workspace and suggest new languages for future support.

## Select Your Workspace Language

Your MoEngage workspace language is set to English by default, but you can change it to any language supported by MoEngage.

<Info>
  MoEngage currently supports Japanese.
</Info>

<Note>
  This is an Early Access feature. To enable it for your account, contact your CSM or [raise a support ticket](/user-guide/contact-support/contact-support/raise-a-support-ticket-through-moengage-dashboard).
</Note>

1. On the **My profile** page, click the **Preferences** tab. The Language preferences page appears.
2. In the **Workspace language** list, select your preferred language. <img alt="Language preference" src="/images/moengage_e59a28.png" />

Your workspace switches to the language you selected.

## Suggest a Language

You can suggest a language if you do not find your preferred language in the **Workspace language** list.

1. Click **Suggest a language**. The Suggest a language dialog box appears. <img src="/images/moengage_e8e46d.png" alt="Suggest a language dialog" />
2. In the **Select preferred language** list, select the language you want MoEngage to support.
3. Click **Done**.

You will receive a notification after MoEngage starts supporting your preferred language.

## Select Your Workspace Appearance

You can personalize your MoEngage UI by choosing a workspace theme that suits your environment. MoEngage offers light and dark modes to reduce eye strain and improve readability based on your preference.

1. Navigate to the MoEngage UI.
2. Use one of the following options:
   - In the upper-right corner, click your profile icon and then click **Appearance**.
     <Frame>
       ![Appearance option](/images/1-1.png)
     </Frame>
   - Click **My profile**.
     <Frame>
       ![My profile option](/images/2-1.png)
     </Frame>
   - On the **My profile** page, click the **Appearance** tab.
     <Frame>
       ![Appearance tab](/images/3-1.png)
     </Frame>
3. In the **Default theme** section, select one of the following options:
   - **Default**: Applies the standard MoEngage light theme.
     <Frame>
       ![Default theme](/images/4-1.png)
     </Frame>
   - **Dark**: Applies a high-contrast dark theme for low-light environments.
     <Frame>
       ![Dark theme](/images/5.png)
     </Frame>
   - **Sync with browser (Light/Dark)**: Automatically adjusts the theme to match your system or browser settings.
     <Frame>
       ![Sync with browser](/images/6.png)
     </Frame>

<Note>
  You can also quickly toggle between light and dark modes by clicking the icon in the top navigation bar next to the notification bell.

  - Moon icon to switch to dark mode:
    <Frame>
      ![Moon icon](/images/7.png)
    </Frame>
  - Sun icon to switch to light mode:
    <Frame>
      ![Sun icon](/images/8.png)
    </Frame>
</Note>
