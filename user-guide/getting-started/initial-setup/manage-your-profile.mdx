MoEngage allows you to manage your profile for each workspace in the MoEngage dashboard. The **My Profile** section has the following tabs:

- [General](#h_01JSCD8A91HAMWQB7XG1D1PQ60)
- [Security](#h_01JSCEAE3GRA3JF09KH82F637V)
- [Preferences](#h_01JSCEAE3G3D6096T5BWZR2GMX)

# General

Under the **General** tab, you can edit your personal information for the current workspace.

1. In the upper-right corner, click your profile icon and then click **My Profile**. <img src="/images/moengage_4abf33.png" alt="Screenshot 2025-04-22 at 6.37.04 PM.png" />
2. On the **My profile** page, the **General** tab is selected by default.
3. Click **Edit** in the upper-right corner. <img src="/images/moengage_25c22a.png" alt="Screenshot 2025-04-22 at 6.39.29 PM.png" />
4. Add your profile picture:
   1. Hover over your profile icon and click the edit icon. <img src="/images/moengage_0ca52c.png" />
   2. Click **Update**.
   3. Select your profile picture and click **Open**.
      <Info>
        MoEngage supports only the .jpeg, .jpg, and .png files.
      </Info>
5. Edit the following details:
   | Field | Description |
   | --- | --- |
   | Name | Enter your name. |
   | Email Id | This field is read-only. |
   | Role | This field is read-only. |
   | Active Since | This field is read-only. |
   | Job title | Enter your job title. |
   | Department | Enter your department name. |
   | Phone number | Select your country code and enter your phone number. |
   | Date of birth | Select your date of birth using the date picker. |
6. Click **Save**. Your personal information is updated.

# Security

The **Security** tab provides tools for enhancing security for the current workspace.

1. In the upper-right corner, click your profile icon and then click **My Profile**. <img alt="Screenshot 2025-04-22 at 6.37.04 PM.png" src="/images/moengage_4abf33.png" />
2. On the **My profile** page, click **Security**. <img src="/images/moengage_1fde0f.png" />
3. You can perform the following tasks:
   - [Change your password](#h_01JSCEAE3GF7CZTV8N46JNAAF2)
   - [Manage 2-step verification](#h_01JSGHTMCAD3RX7TX4XZJ63X63)
   - [Log out of your active devices](#h_01JSCEAE3G595W5Y3681A2XGSB)

## Change Your Password

To change your password, perform the following steps:

1. On the **Security** tab, click **Change password** in the upper-right corner of the **Password** section. <img src="/images/moengage_48bf1e.png" alt="Screenshot 2025-04-22 at 6.51.05 PM.png" />
2. Enter the following details:
   | Field | Description |
   | --- | --- |
   | Old password | Enter the old or existing password. |
   | New password | Enter the new password. Ensure the password meets the following criteria: <ul><li>Contains 8 to 20 characters.</li><li>Contains one capital letter.</li><li>Contains one number.</li><li>Contains one special character among !@#\$%^&\*()\_?/,`<>`;:`{}` ~ and no space</li></ul> |
   | Retype new password | Retype the new password. |
3. Click **Save**. Your password is updated.

## Manage 2-Step Verification

MoEngage allows you to secure your MoEngage account (workspace) with 2-step verification. This helps you reduce the risk of unauthorized access due to compromised passwords. Your Admin can enable it for all users at the account (workspace) level. For more information, refer to [2-Step Verification or Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)](/user-guide/settings/account/security/2-step-verification-or-two-factor-authentication-2fa). However, you can manage it at the user level.

### Set up 2-Step Verification for Your Workspace

<Info>
  The **Setup device** option appears when both of the following criteria are met:

  - If your Admin has disabled 2-step verification at the account (workspace) level.
  - You log in to your account for the first time.
</Info>

1. On the My profile page, click **Security**. <img src="/images/moengage_9e14ca.png" />
2. Click **Setup device** adjacent to **2-step verification**. <img src="/images/moengage_c76b87.png" /> The Set up your device authentication pop-up window appears. <img src="/images/moengage_58eb44.png" alt="setup.png" />
3. Open the authenticator app now (for example, Google Authenticator) and tap the **\+** icon.
   <img
     src="/images/moengage_3dbec7.png"
     style={{ width:"35%" }}
   />
   The Add an authentication code screen appears.
   <img
     src="/images/moengage_dad2a7.png"
     style={{ width:"38%" }}
   />
4. Use one of the following authentication options:
   - **Using a QR code**
     1. Tap **Scan a QR code**. The Scan code screen appears.
        <img
          src="/images/moengage_416a39.png"
          style={{ width:"35%" }}
        />
     2. Scan the QR code on the Set up your device authentication pop-up window in the MoEngage dashboard. An authentication is added to your device. Your authentication app generates a 6-digit code.
   - **Using a setup key**
     1. Tap **Enter a setup key**.
        <img
          src="/images/moengage_c6bdf9.png"
          style={{ width:"41%" }}
        />
     2. On the **Enter account details** screen, enter the following details :
        | Fields | Required | Description |
        | --- | --- | --- |
        | Account name | Optional | Type your account name. |
        | Your key | Yes | Type the 32-digit secret key displayed on the Set up your device authentication pop-up window in the MoEngage dashboard.  <img alt="setup.png" src="/images/moengage_58eb44.png" /> |
        | Type of key | Optional | This is the type of your key. Supported values are: <ul><li>**Time based**: The OTP is generated based on the current time.</li><li>**Counter based**: The  OTP is generated based on an incrementing counter.</li></ul> Select an option. In the following example, _Time based_ is selected. |
        <img
          src="/images/moengage_f6ebb2.png"
          style={{ width:"45%" }}
        />
     3. Tap **Add**.
     4. An authentication is added to your device with the name you entered in the **Account name** box. Your authentication app generates a 6-digit code.
        <img
          src="/images/moengage_20e2ed.png"
          style={{ width:"48%" }}
        />
5. In the **Enter the 6-digit verification code below** box, type the 6-digit authentication code generated by your authentication app.
   <Info>
     Each authentication code your authentication app generates is valid only for 30 seconds. After that, your authentication app generates a new code.
   </Info>
   ![setup copy.png](/images/moengage_e5d748.png)
6. Click **Submit**.\
   You have successfully configured 2-step verification on your device for your MoEngage account.

### Enable 2-Step Verification for Your Workspace

<Info>
  The **Enable device** option appears only if your admin has disabled 2FA at the account (workspace) level and you have not enabled it for your workspace.
</Info>

To enable 2FA for the current workspace, perform the following steps:

1. On the **My profile** page, click **Security**. <img src="/images/moengage_9e14ca.png" />
2. Click the ellipse icon adjacent to **2 step verification** and then click **Enable device**. <img src="/images/moengage_1379cd.png" /> The Enable device pop-up window appears, prompting you for confirmation to enable 2FA for your workspace.
3. Click **Enable**. <img src="/images/moengage_a0e357.png" />\
   The 2FA is now successfully enabled for your workspace.

After you enable 2FA for your workspace, you will be automatically logged out of the MoEngage dashboard. When you log in to the MoEngage dashboard again, you must set up 2-step verification on your device. For more information, refer to [Set Up 2-Step Verification on Your Device](/user-guide/getting-started/initial-setup/set-up-2-step-verification-on-your-device).

### Remove 2-Step Verification for the Workspace

<Info>
  The **Remove device** option is visible only when both of the following criteria are met:

  - You have enabled 2FA for your workspace.
  - Your admin disables it at the account (workspace) level.
</Info>

To remove 2FA for the current workspace, perform the following steps:

1. On the **My profile** page, click **Security**. <img src="/images/moengage_9e14ca.png" />
2. Click the ellipse icon adjacent to **2 step verification** and then click **Remove** **device**. <img src="/images/moengage_f7608e.png" /> The Remove device pop-up window appears, prompting you for confirmation to remove 2FA for your workspace.
3. Click **Remove**. <img src="/images/moengage_d964c4.png" />\
   The 2FA is now successfully removed from your workspace.

## Log Out of Your Active Devices

Under **Active devices**, you can see all the devices you have used to access this workspace. Perform the following steps to log out of a device:

1. Click the logout icon adjacent to the active device from which you want to log out. Your device is successfully logged out. <img src="/images/moengage_23de27.png" alt="Screenshot 2025-04-22 at 6.10.10 PM.png" />
2. Click the ellipsis icon and then click **Logout of all devices** to log out of all your active devices. <img src="/images/moengage_19a4f9.png" alt="Screenshot 2025-04-22 at 6.11.19 PM.png" /> The Logout pop-up window will appear, prompting you to confirm.
3. Click **Logout**. <img src="/images/moengage_db7bcc.png" />

# Preferences

Under the **Preferences** tab, you can select your preferred language for the MoEngage workspace and suggest new languages for future support.

## Select Your Workspace Language

Your MoEngage workspace language is set to English by default, but you can change it to any language supported by MoEngage.

<Info>
  MoEngage currently supports Japanese.
</Info>

<Note>
  This is an Early Access feature. To enable it for your account, contact your CSM or [raise a support ticket](/user-guide/contact-support/contact-support/raise-a-support-ticket-through-moengage-dashboard).
</Note>

Perform the following steps to select the workspace language:

1. On the **My profile** page, click the **Preferences** tab. The Language preferences page appears.
2. In the **Workspace language** list, select your preferred language. <img alt="language preference.png" src="/images/moengage_e59a28.png" />

Your workspace switches to the language you selected.

## Suggest a Language

You can suggest a language if you do not find your preferred language in the **Workspace language** list. Perform the following steps to suggest a language:

1. Click **Suggest a language**. The Suggest a language dialog box appears. <img src="/images/moengage_e8e46d.png" alt="Screenshot 2025-04-22 at 6.33.02 PM.png" />
2. In the **Select preferred language** list, select the language you want MoEngage to support.
3. Click **Done**.

You will receive a notification after MoEngage starts supporting your preferred language.
