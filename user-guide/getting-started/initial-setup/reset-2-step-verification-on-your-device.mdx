---
title: "Reset 2-Step Verification on Your Device"
---

MoEngage allows you to reset 2-step verification in the following scenarios:

- You are unable to authenticate yourself using the verification code generated by the current authenticator app.
- You have used all recovery codes and want to generate a new set of codes.

<Tip>
You must have set up 2-step verification on your device. For more information, refer to [Set Up 2-Step Verification on Your Device](https://help.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/36908840992276).
</Tip>

To reset 2-step verification on your device, perform the following steps:

1. Try to [log in to your account](https://help.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/34852261258004) using your credentials, based on the login method configured for the account.
2. On the 2-Step Verification page, click **Receive reset 2-step verification link via email**.  
    <img  src="/images/moengage_d1089b.png"  />
3. On the 2-Step Verification page, click **Send link**.  
    <img alt="2fa reset.png" src="/images/moengage_60b241.png" />   
   A login link to reset your 2FA is now sent to your email.
4. Open the email sent by MoEngage and click **here** **to reset your 2FA credentials**.  
    <img  src="/images/moengage_b61f2f.png"  />   
   After clicking, you are taken to the Login to your accounts page in a new tab on your browser.  
    <img alt="Screenshot 2025-05-06 at 3.35.14 PM.png"  src="/images/moengage_529598.png"  />
5. Log in to your account using your credentials, based on the login method configured for the account. You are taken to the 2-Step Verification page.
6. Click **Setup your device**.  
    <img  src="/images/moengage_2a04b3.png"  />   
   The Set up your device authentication page appears. You must reset 2FA on your device.  
    <img alt="8 copy 2.png" src="/images/moengage_d75795.png" />
7. Open the authenticator app on your device now (for example, Google Authenticator) and tap the  **<img  src="/images/moengage_8e66e0.png"  />** icon.  
    <img  src="/images/moengage_a1ad95.png"  />   
   The Add an authentication code screen appears.  
    <img  src="/images/moengage_67a64b.png"  />
8. Use one of the following authentication options:
   - **Using a QR code**
     1. Tap **Scan a QR code**. The Scan code screen appears.  
         <img  src="/images/moengage_f2d3e7.png"  />
     2. Scan the QR code on the **Set your device authentication** page. An authentication is added to your device. Your authentication app generates a 6-digit code
   - **Using a setup key**
     1. Tap **Enter a setup key.  
         <img  src="/images/moengage_c6eca4.png"  />**
     2. On the **Enter account details** screen, enter the following details:

        | Fields | Required | Description |
        | --- | --- | --- |
        | Account name | Optional | Type your account name. |
        | Your key | Yes | Type the 32-digit secret key displayed on the **Set your device authentication** page.  <img alt="8 copy.png" src="/images/moengage_504126.png" /> |
        | Type of key | Optional | This is the type of your key. Supported values are:  - **Time based**: The OTP is generated based on the current time. - **Counter based**: The  OTP is generated based on an incrementing counter. Select an option. In the following example, *Time based* is selected. |

          <img  src="/images/moengage_006789.png"  />
     3. Tap **Add**.  
        An authentication is added to your device with the name you entered in the **Account name** box. Your device receives a new authentication, which appears below the previous authentication.  
         <img  src="/images/moengage_b32dba.png"  />   
        In the future, you must use the verification code from the new instance only.
9. In the **Enter the 6 digit verification code** box, type the 6-digit authentication code generated by your authentication app.
   <Info>
Each authentication code your authentication app generates is valid only for 30 seconds. After that, your authentication app generates a new code.
</Info>
   <img  src="/images/moengage_7303d7.png"  />
10. Click **Confirm**. You have successfully reset the 2-step verification on your device.
11. Click **Download recovery codes** to access your account without your device. The recovery codes will be downloaded in the CSV file.
    <Info>
You should store these codes securely and not share them with others. They serve as a backup or alternative method to verify your identity if you lose access to your primary 2-step verification device. For more information, refer to [Recovery Codes](https://help.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/36908631897876).
</Info>
    <img src="/images/moengage_6241c0.png" />
12. Click **Login**. The Login to your accounts page appears.  
     <img alt="10 copy.png" src="/images/moengage_ae816f.png" />
13. You can log in to your account now. For more information, refer to [Log in to MoEngage](https://help.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/34852261258004).
