## Problem

The payload for Card campaigns failed to be fetched.

## Explanation

For the payload to be fetched:

- The card campaigns must be active.
- The campaigns must be sent out.
- The **Show Card** toggle must be turned on.

## Example

- In the following example, there are two Card campaigns: one is active, and the other is paused. <img alt="two cards.png" src="/images/moengage_41c105.png" />
- The sent and delivered counts for the active campaign are 0 because the campaign is not sent to anyone. <img alt="active cards.png" src="/images/moengage_593222.png" />
- You can fetch the payload only if the Card campaign is sent and the **Show Card** toggle is turned on.
