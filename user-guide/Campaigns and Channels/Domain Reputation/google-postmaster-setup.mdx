This article helps you understand the process of setting up a Google Postmaster account, which is a mandatory step in receiving [domain reputation](https://help.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/19679680443284) data in MoEngage UI. The following are the steps to be performed to set up a Google Postmaster account.

1. [Configure Google Postmaster](https://support.google.com/a/answer/9981691?hl=en&visit_id=638589862558411978-3959488909&rd=2#) on your end.
2. Share Google Postmaster [READ access](https://support.google.com/a/answer/9981691?hl=en&visit_id=638589862558411978-3959488909&rd=2#) of domains with the [Google Postmaster account of MoEngage](mailto:googlepostmoe@moengage.com).

# Configure Google Postmaster

Perform the following steps to configure the Google Postmaster on your end:

1. Log in to your Gmail account using a browser.
2. Navigate to [Google Postmaster Tools](https://postmaster.google.com/u/0/managedomains?pli=1).
3. Click the **Add** (+) button on the bottom right corner.
4. Add the root domain (example.com).
5. Copy the **TXT** or **CNAME** records for the domain.
6. Go to your DNS server and add either the TXT or the CNAME record.
7. Go to Google Postmaster and verify the domain. Note that the published DNS record might take up to 24 hours to populate.
8. After the root domain (example.com) is validated, click the **Add** (+) icon to add all the subdomains used for emails. Note that if the root domains are already validated, subdomains get validated without adding any additional DNS records.

## Share the READ Access with the Google Postmaster Account of MoEngage

Perform the following steps in your Google Postmaster account to share read access of domains with the Google Postmaster account of MoEngage.

1. Log in to your Google Postmaster account.
2. Hover over the validated domain and click <img alt="ellipsis 1.png"  src="/images/moengage_b8697a.png"  />
3. Click **Manage Users**.
4. Click**Add** + on the bottom right corner.
5. Add the Google Postmaster account of MoEngage and click **Next**. Note that the Google Postmaster account of MoEngage is [googlepostmoe@moengage.com](mailto:googlepostmoe@moengage.com).
6. Repeat the previous steps for all domains and subdomains.
