## Problem

When you edit a campaign without global control group settings, the **Global control group** toggle is turned on by default.

## Solution

- On the Campaign Info page, you will see **Global Control Group: Disabled** for campaigns that do not have this option enabled.  
   <img  src="/images/moengage_d31aa1.png"  />
- When editing the same campaign, the **Global control group** toggle is automatically turned on.  
   <img  src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfVNC53Owvrp4ANoMuObnAVyEUQdESmyJ1oel9VdHjWJ9oydKDEeuUOcw1XNBf8hSf_p4ysMhu44YQVk_m_p6p1vQl03MAX-LIQUdv1h2Ys5H88FKhn8b7-Lsb6OJKmD9ZRrY87eoHKnIrSHp5HmNICwIs?key=g0z_80HMO2h2dAuqsAGugQ"  />
- This is an expected behavior because the *apply\_to\_existing\_campaigns* condition is set to true, turning the **Global control group** toggle on during campaign editing.
