# Introduction

[Snowflake](https://docs.snowflake.com/en/user-guide-getting-started) is a data platform that allows organizations to store, analyze, and process large volumes of structured and semi-structured data in a highly scalable and efficient manner. With its unique architecture, Snowflake allows organizations to consolidate their data, perform quick analytics, and gain valuable data-driven insights accessible to all users.

# MoEngage `<>` Snowflake

The MoEngage and Snowflake integration exports Campaign Interaction Events data directly to your Snowflake tables.
