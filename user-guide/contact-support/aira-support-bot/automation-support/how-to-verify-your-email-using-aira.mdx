title: "How To Verify Your Email Using AIRA?"
---

Verifying your email address used to be a slow, manual process. Now, it’s quick and seamless. You can complete this quickly using the AIRA bot on your dashboard.

## Prerequisites

- Access to the MoEngage dashboard.
- The email address that you want to verify.

## Instructions

Perform the following steps to verify your email using the AIRA directly from the **MoEngage dashboard**:

1. To launch the AIRA, click on **Need help** and then select **Support.**

   <Frame>
     <img
       src="/images/support-email.png"
       alt="Support Email"
       title="Support Email"
       style={{ width:"58%" }}
     />
   </Frame>
2. Click on **Ask a question,** and in the chat window, type “Verify email”.

   <img
     src="/images/moengage_951677.png"
     style={{ width:"53%" }}
   />

   <Info>
     You can use any prompts that indicate you want to verify your email. The options listed below are just a few examples to help you begin the email verification process:
     1. Need to verify email IDs.
     2. Verify the email.
     3. Verify a new email address.
     4. Email verification pending.
   </Info>
1. AIRA will guide you with the next steps and trigger the verification process.
2. Once verified, you will see a confirmation from AIRA in the chat and on the dashboard.

   <Info>
     - Ensure the dashboard page is refreshed.
     - If there are multiple email senders, AIRA will prompt you to select the one you want to verify.
     - After completing the process, take a moment to rate your experience with AIRA.
   </Info>
