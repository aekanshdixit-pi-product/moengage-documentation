---
title: "Why Is It Not Possible to Select Different Connectors in Email Connector Settings?"
---

## Problem

It is not possible to select different connectors from the **Email Connector** drop-down list.

## Explanation

The Email Connector drop-down list displays only those email connectors with an associated _From_ email address. If a connector does not have a _From_ address configured, it will not be available for selection.
