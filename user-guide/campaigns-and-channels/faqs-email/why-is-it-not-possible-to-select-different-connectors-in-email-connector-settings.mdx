---
title: "Why Is It Not Possible to Select Different Connectors in Email Connector Settings?"
---

## Problem

It is not possible to select different connectors from the **Email Connector** drop-down list.

## Explanation

The Email Connector drop-down list displays only those email connectors with an associated *F**rom* email address. If a connector does not have a *From* address configured, it will not be available for selection. <img alt="email connector.png" src="/images/moengage_ae0e59.png" />
