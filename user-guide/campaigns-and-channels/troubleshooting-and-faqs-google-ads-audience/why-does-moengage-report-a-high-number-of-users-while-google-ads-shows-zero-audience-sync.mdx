## Solution

When the number of users synced from MoEngage to Google Ads is less than 4000, the Google Ads segment will show zero. For more information, refer to [Google Ads Help](https://support.google.com/google-ads/thread/197578971/ga-ga4-linked-audiences-not-populating-in-google-ads?hl=en).
