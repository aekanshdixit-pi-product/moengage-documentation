Connecting with the right people can improve the return on your Google Ads spending. MoEngage Google Ads Audience Sync helps you reach and retarget users who have shown interest in your brand or product but haven't taken action, like signing up or making a purchase.

## What is Google Ads Audience Sync?

Audiences are a group or collection of users with similar interests or who have expressed similar intents. By creating an audience, you can reach out to potential customers.

Google Ads Audience Sync allows you to send user segments directly from MoEngage to Google Ads for targeted campaigns. This feature works with [Google Ads Customer Match](https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/6299717), which helps you reach potential customers more effectively.

![Google\_Ads\_Audience\_Flow.png](/images/moengage_e34134.png)

Customer Match helps you target users who have interacted with your app or product and demonstrated a strong buying intent. By reaching these specific users with relevant ads, you can increase conversions.

To use Customer Match, Google Ads requires you to upload specific user data. This data helps Google identify and match your users. You must upload one of the following types of user data, hashed using SHA256 for security:

- Email ID
- Mobile Number
- Device ID (Apple IDFA or Android AAID)

<Info>
  You can use Customer Match only when you meet the criteria in [Google's Customer Match Policy](https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/6299717).
</Info>

Google Ads Audience Sync helps you manage your customer match lists by allowing you to:

- Create a new customer match list and send a user segment.
- Add users to an existing customer match list.
- Remove users from an existing customer match list.

This feature helps you target users in real-time and exclude them from ad campaigns as soon as they complete a desired action, like making a purchase.

## Updated EU User Consent Policy of Google

Google's updated [EU User Consent Policy](https://www.google.com/about/company/user-consent-policy/), which came into effect on March 6, 2024, responds to changes in the [Digital Markets Act (DMA)](https://ads-developers.googleblog.com/2023/10/updates-to-customer-match-conversion.html). This policy requires advertisers to do the following for their users in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom (UK):

- Disclose when you use cookies or other local storage, if legally required.
- Obtain consent to collect, share, and use their personal data for ad personalization.

## How MoEngage Helps You Comply

MoEngage Google Ads Audience Sync helps you comply with Google's new EU User Consent Policy when you send EU customer data from MoEngage to Google Ads. MoEngage allows you to sync audiences from third-party sources to Google Ads Customer Match audiences.

MoEngage uses the following standard user attributes to manage consent:

- **Consent for ad user data**: This tracks consent for sending user data to Google for advertising.
- **Consent for ad personalization**: This tracks consent for personalized advertising.

Both parameters support the following values:

| Value | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Granted | Users have given permission to send ads. |
| Denied | Users have denied permission to send ads. |
| Unspecified | Consent information for these users is not available. |

## Actions for Marketers: What You Need to Do

If you have customers in the EU region, you must get their permission before sending them ads. If you do not have customers in the EU region, you do not need to take any action.

<Info>
  This information applies only if you have customers in the EU region. If not, you do not need to take any action.
</Info>

To serve ads successfully to your customers, complete these steps:

1. Collect consent from your customers. This usually involves implementing a Consent Management Platform (CMP) on your website or app.
2. Update the consent attributes in MoEngage with the collected permission values. You can do this through the [MoEngage User Attribute API](/api/user/track-user) or other data import methods.
3. When you create a user segment for your target audience, add another query to include the relevant consent parameters. For detailed instructions, refer to [Create a Google Ads Audience Campaign](/user-guide/campaigns-and-channels/google-ads-audience/create/create-a-google-ads-audience-campaign).
