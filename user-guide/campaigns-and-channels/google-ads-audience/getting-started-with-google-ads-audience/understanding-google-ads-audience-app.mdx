---
title: "Understanding Google Ads Audience App"
---

The MoEngage Google Ads Audience app enables you to sync your customer data with Google Ads for precise audience targeting. This integration helps you use your customer insights directly within Google Ads campaigns, which enhances your marketing efforts.

The app accesses your Customer Match audiences in Google Ads. It only fetches your custom audiences and helps add or remove customers from existing audiences. The app does not store or modify other data, such as ads or analytics.

## Key Benefits

- **Automated Audience Syncing:** Automatically synchronize customer segments from MoEngage to Google Ads, eliminating the need for manual file uploads.
- **Enhanced Targeting:** Use rich behavioral data from MoEngage to create highly relevant audiences in Google Ads.
- **Improved Campaign Performance:** Deliver personalized ad experiences to drive better engagement and a higher return on investment (ROI).
- **Efficiency:** Set up periodic or event-triggered audience updates. This ensures you always have fresh data for your campaigns without manual effort.

## How the Google Ads Audience App Works

The MoEngage Google Ads Audience app connects directly to your Google Ads account. It identifies and synchronizes user segments (also known as cohorts) that you define in MoEngage based on your customers' behavior within your app.

This app helps you manage audiences by allowing MoEngage to:

- Add new customers to existing Google Ads audiences.
- Remove customers from existing Google Ads audiences.

The synchronization can occur periodically or trigger automatically based on specific user actions within your app. This ensures your Google Ads audiences are always up to date.

## Before You Begin

To use the Google Ads Audience app, you must have:

- An active MoEngage account.
- Access to a Google Ads account with permissions to manage Customer Match audiences.
