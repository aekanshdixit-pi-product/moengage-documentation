---
title: "Test Connectors"
---

To test whether your Connector has been configured properly, you can use [Beeceptor](https://beeceptor.com/) or similar tools. Beeceptor gives you a URL that accepts events and allows you to see the events and payloads that MoEngage sends. To test your connector configuration, do the following:

1. Go to [Beeceptor.com](https://beeceptor.com/), add your subdomain, and click **Create Endpoint.** <img src="/images/moengage_dd61e2.png" alt="Screen_Shot_2022-04-06_at_11.29.08_AM.png" />
2. Copy the Endpoint/URL
    <img src="/images/moengage_277910.png" alt="Screen_Shot_2022-04-06_at_11.29.00_AM.png" />
3. [Create the connector campaign](/user-guide/campaigns-and-channels/connectors/create/create-a-connector-campaign) and paste this URL in place of the Webhook URL. <img alt="Screen_Shot_2022-04-06_at_11.33.35_AM.png" src="/images/moengage_4ec15b.png" />
4. Create the request with the parameters you want to test. MoEngage will show if the request was successful.
   ![Screen\_Shot\_2022-04-06\_at\_11.36.01\_AM.png](/images/moengage_c006df.png)
5. As soon as the request is triggered, you should be able to see the complete report in Beeceptor against your end-point.
   ![Screen\_Shot\_2022-04-06\_at\_11.37.05\_AM.png](/images/moengage_2b512d.png)
6. Click to expand and view the complete request.
   ![Screen\_Shot\_2022-04-06\_at\_11.37.31\_AM.png](/images/moengage_5e067b.png)
