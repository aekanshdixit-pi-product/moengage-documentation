---
title: "Test Connectors"
---

To test whether your Connector has been configured properly, you can use [Beeceptor](https://beeceptor.com/) or similar tools. Beeceptor gives you a URL that accepts events and allows you to see the events and payloads that MoEngage sends. To test your connector configuration, do the following:

1. 1. 1. Go to [Beeceptor.com](https://beeceptor.com/), add your subdomain, and click on **Create Endpoint.**

         <img alt="Screen_Shot_2022-04-06_at_11.29.08_AM.png"  src="/images/moengage_dd61e2.png"  />
      2. Copy the Endpoint/URL

         <img alt="Screen_Shot_2022-04-06_at_11.29.00_AM.png"  src="/images/moengage_277910.png"  />
      3. [Create the connector campaign](http://help.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/214247906)and paste this URL in place of the Webhook URL. <img alt="Screen_Shot_2022-04-06_at_11.33.35_AM.png"  src="/images/moengage_4ec15b.png"  />
      4. Create the request with the parameters you want to test. MoEngage will show if the request was successful.

         <img alt="Screen_Shot_2022-04-06_at_11.36.01_AM.png"  src="/images/moengage_c006df.png"  />
      5. As soon as the request is triggered, you should be able to see the complete report in Beeceptor against your end-point.

         <img alt="Screen_Shot_2022-04-06_at_11.37.05_AM.png" src="/images/moengage_2b512d.png" />
      6. Click to expand and view the complete request.

         <img alt="Screen_Shot_2022-04-06_at_11.37.31_AM.png" src="/images/moengage_5e067b.png" />
