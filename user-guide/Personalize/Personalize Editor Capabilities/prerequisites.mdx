The Personalize Editor allows you to tailor your application interface, elements, themes, and widgets to suit your business standards, workflows, and preferences.

You can use the Personalize Editor in step 2 of your [Create Experience](https://help.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/11621615057300-Create-Experience#h_01HKST0QQEDP9CD61CVS6DQ1AF) workflow. In this article, we will learn about the Personalize Editor features and capabilities in detail.

<Tip>
- Ensure that you've navigated to your MoEngage dashboard and initiated creating an Experience.
- Ensure that you've entered your webpage address for personalization. To learn more, see [Create Experience](https://help.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/11621615057300-Create-Experience#h_01HKST0QQEDP9CD61CVS6DQ1AF).
</Tip>
