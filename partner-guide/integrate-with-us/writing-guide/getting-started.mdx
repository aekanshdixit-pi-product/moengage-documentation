## Almost There! Let's Document Your Integration.

You've built and tested your integration! Congratulations, the finish line is in sight! This guide is the final, crucial step to getting your app listed in the MoEngage App Marketplace and into the hands of our mutual customers.

Think of this as crafting the story of your integration. Following our templates will help you create a clear, compelling guide that ensures a fantastic user experience and significantly speeds up the publishing process. Let's stick the landing!

## How to Use This Guide

Follow the steps below to use the template and create clear, comprehensive documentation for your integration:

  1. **Read the General Guidelines** : Familiarize yourself with our tone, style, and formatting requirements in [General Documentation Guidelines](https://partners.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/41371357841940).
  2. **Begin with the Universal Sections** : Complete the "Overview," "Use Cases," "Prerequisites," and other standard sections ([Partner Guide Structure](https://partners.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/41372280491796)) for your integration.
  3. **Find Your Category Example** : Go to [Category-Specific Templates](https://partners.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/41434314506388). Find the category that best matches your integration (for example, SMS Provider, Data and Infrastructure, and so on).
  4. **Structure Your Integration Setup** : Use the example for your category as a model to write your step-by-step setup instructions. The examples show the level of detail, structure, and type of information MoEngage needs.
