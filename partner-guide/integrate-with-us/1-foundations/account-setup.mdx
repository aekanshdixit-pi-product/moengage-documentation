---
title: "Account setup"
---

You'll need to set up a MoEngage Sandbox Workspace in order to get started. If you already have your own MoEngage account and want to use that for the integration, then [log in here](https://dashboard.moengage.com/v4/#/auth). 

Alternatively, you can [sign up](https://dashboard-01.moengage.com/v4/#/auth/signup) to create your own account for your integration and testing. The new account creation is completely free.

These free accounts that we provide you will have some basic capabilities of a standard MoEngage production account: 

  1. Send data (users/event/segments) to MoEngage.
  2. View your data on your MoEngage dashboard.
  3. [Invite team members](https://help.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041804831-Manage-Members) to collaborate and integrate
  4. Access to basic functionalities of our engagement suite. 



Reach out to us at [partnerships@moengage.com](mailto:partnerships@moengage.com), and we will enable additional capabilities required for you to build and test your integration:

  1. MoEngage Streams
  2. Connected Apps
