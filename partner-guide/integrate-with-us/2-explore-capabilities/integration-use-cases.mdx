# Introduction 

The MoEngage Partner Ecosystem is a trusted network of technology partners. This best-of-breed ecosystem thrives on empowering consumer brands like yours to up your Customer Engagement game.

# Category wise use-cases

Here's how different type of partners have become an integral part of our ecosystem. 

## 1\. MoEngage × Customer Data Platforms

Achieving customer-centricity involves a two-pronged approach. The first step is gathering insights about the users and their behavior, and the second step is leveraging these insights to create personalized experiences both on and off your platform.

Customer Data Platforms (CDPs) ensure that your data is consistent, centralized, and easily accessible. MoEngage lets you send relevant communication to these users across multiple channels.

### Benefits 

  * Send data from CDP to MoEngage in a few steps without any dev effort. 
  * Enrich user profiles with their behavior on your platform, interests, preferences, and affinity. 
  * Predict customer cohorts that are likely to hibernate, churn, or convert
  * Orchestrate cross-channel customer journeys to ensure efficient onboarding or increase LTV

## 4\. MoEngage × Dynamic content platforms

Marketers use creatives such as images, gifs, real time elements in their campaigns to make visually appealing and hence increase their campaigns' conversions. MoEngage allows marketers to use the creatives that created on external tools as URLs in MoEngage campaigns. 

### Benefits

You can create personalized content for every customer at the time of engagement. For example:

  * Use the Countdown Timer feature to notify users when a big sale is approaching. 
  * Use the Scratch Off feature as a fun and interactive way to disburse Promo Codes.
  * Use unique and visually appealing creatives (images/ gifs) in MoEngage campaigns.
