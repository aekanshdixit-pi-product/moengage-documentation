# Introduction

[Google Ads Audience ](https://business.google.com/en-all/google-ads/)sync enables you to reach customers by using the data you collect in MoEngage. By synchronizing your MoEngage segments with Google Ads Audience, you can create highly relevant advertising campaigns that resonate with users across Google Search, YouTube, and the Google Display Network.

## MoEngage <> Google Ads Audience

By using MoEngage for Google Ads Audience Sync, you can synchronize first-party user data from the MoEngage platform to Google Customer Match. This allows you to deliver advertisements based on behavioral triggers, lifecycle stages, and custom segments.

Any criteria you typically use to trigger a message (such as Push, Email, or SMS) within a MoEngage campaign can now be used to trigger targeted advertisements to specific users through Google Ads.

## Use Cases

Integrating Google Ads Audience with MoEngage helps you with the following use cases:

  * Target high-value users through multiple channels to drive purchases and engagement
  * Retarget users who are less responsive on other marketing channels.
  * Create a suppression audience to prevent users from receiving advertisements if they are already loyal consumers of your brand.
  * Create lookalike audiences to acquire new users more efficiently.



**Note** : This integration allows you to control which specific first-party data is shared with Google.

# Integration

| library_add_check |  **Prerequisites** Ensure that you have the following:

  * **An active Google Ads account** : Required to manage your advertising campaigns.
  * **Google Customer Match eligibility** : Your account must comply with Google Customer Match policies to use audience synchronization.
  * **Administrative permissions** : You must have administrative access to the Google Ads account you plan to link with MoEngage to grant the necessary authorization.
  * **Compliance with terms** : You must accept the Google Customer Match Terms within your Google Ads dashboard before you initiate the sync.
