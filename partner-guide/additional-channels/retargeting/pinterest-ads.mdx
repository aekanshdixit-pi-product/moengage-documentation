[Pinterest Ads](https://www.pinterest.com/) allows businesses to use various targeting options to reach specific audiences, including those who have previously visited their website, engaged with their Pins, or are interested in similar products or topics.  
  
# MoEngage <> Pinterest Ads

The MoEngage and Pinterest integration empowers you to seamlessly transfer your customer data to Pinterest Ads. Utilizing this data, you can create targeted advertising lists based on specific user behaviors and segmentation, leading to more relevant ad delivery.

## Use Cases

Integrating Pinterest with MoEngage helps you solve the following use cases:

  * **Enhanced user engagement and conversions** : You can target high-value users via multiple channels to drive purchases or engagement.
  * **Re-engagement of less responsive users** : You can target users who are less responsive to other marketing channels by showing them targeted display ads based on their past behavior.
  * **Optimized advertising spend** : You can create suppression audiences to prevent users from receiving advertisements when they’re already loyal consumers of your brand.
  * **Efficient new user acquisition** : You can create lookalike audiences to acquire new users more efficiently.

## Step 1: Create a Developer App on Pinterest

To set up a Pinterest developer account, perform the following steps:

  1. Go to the [Pinterest Developers Platform](https://developer.pinterest.com/).
  2. Follow the instructions on the [Pinterest document](https://developers.pinterest.com/docs/getting-started/connect-app/) to set up a new App or use your existing App.
  3. To connect MoEngage to your Pinterest App configuration, type the specific URL in the **Redirect link** box based on the following MoEngage data center.  
![](/images/partner_37662413714580.png)  
| Data Center |  Redirect URI

| --- | --- |  
| DC-01 | `https://dashboard-01.moengage.com/v3/partner/oauth/callback`  
| DC-02 |  `https://dashboard-02.moengage.com/v3/partner/oauth/callback`  
| DC-03 |  `https://dashboard-03.moengage.com/v3/partner/oauth/callback`  
| DC-04 |  `https://dashboard-04.moengage.com/v3/partner/oauth/callback`  
  4. MoEngage would require access to the following scopes:   
| Scope Required |  Use Case

| --- | --- |  
| `ads:write` |  This allows you to create and manage Audiences in Pinterest.  
| `ads:read ` |  This allows you to read existing Audiences on Pinterest.  
  5. From your Pinterest App dashboard, on the **Configure** tab, obtain the **App id** and **App secret key**.  
![](/images/partner_37662413715220.png)

To connect Pinterest on the App marketplace, perform the following steps:

  1. On the left navigation menu in the MoEngage dashboard, click **App marketplace**.
  2. On the App Marketplace page, search for **Pinterest**.

## Step 3: Create a Campaign on MoEngage to Sync Audiences to Pinterest Ads

To create a campaign on MoEngage, perform the following steps:

  1. On the left navigation menu in the MoEngage dashboard, click **Engage** , and then click **Campaigns**.
  2. On the All campaigns page, click**\+ Create campaign**.
  3. On the **Create campaign** dialog box, under **Connected Apps** , click **Pinterest** and then click the most suitable delivery type. ![](/images/partner_38252358672788.png)
  4. Under **Select audience** , click **Filter users by**.
  5. On the **User property** tab, select the user segment for Pinterest syncing. You can combine this with other segment criteria.
  6. In step 2 of the campaign, **Content** , select the pre-configured connectors and specify the content you want to send out. Enter the following details:  

| Field | Description

| --- | --- |  
| **Connector** |  You can perform the following actions:
     * **Create Customer List**
     * **Update Customer List**  
| **Connections** | Select the connection you’ve added to the MoEngage App marketplace.  
![](/images/partner_38252406906132.png)  


Based on the connector you select, you must provide the following information:

Create Customer ListUpdate Customer List

| Field | Description

| --- | --- |  
| **Customer List Name** |  This is the name of your Customer List. This creates a new Customer List with the provided name.  
| **Customer List Type** |  Select the intended identifier type for this new customer list. By default, all identifiers are hashed using SHA-256.  
![](/images/partner_38252358673812.png)

| Field | Description

| --- | --- |  
| **Add or remove users** |  Select whether you want to add or remove users from your Customer List.  
| **Customer List ID** |  This the ID of your Customer List.  
| **Select Identifier Type** |  Select the identifier type to match users on Pinterest. MoEngage supports two identifiers:
     * Email ID
     * Google Advertiser ID.  
| **Identifier Value** |  Type **@** and select the user attribute that contains the identifier value. By default, you can use “Email (Standard)” for Email and “Google Advertiser ID (Android)” for Google Advertiser ID.  
  
![](/images/partner_38252406907028.png)

  7. Your campaign is now ready to be tested and sent to your audience. Using the **Test** functionality, you can also test your connector configuration.  
![](/images/partner_37662413716244.png)If your test user successfully receives the message, your setup is ready.

  8. Publish the campaign.

  
**Note** : If you're creating a new audience, do not publish the campaign.
