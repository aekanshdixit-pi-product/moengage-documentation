[Snapchat Ads](https://forbusiness.snapchat.com/) are full-screen digital advertisements that businesses can use to reach a highly engaged audience on the Snapchat app. Snapchat Ads can help businesses:

  * Create brand awareness
  * Drive website traffic
  * Encourage app installs



# MoEngage × Snapchat 

The integration of MoEngage and Snapchat Ads allows you to synchronize your audience segments on MoEngage with Snapchat. This enables the creation of highly targeted advertising campaigns tailored to specific end users.

## Use Cases

The integration with MoEngage helps you with the following use cases:

  * **Elevating user interaction and sales on Snapchat** : By leveraging detailed user insights from MoEngage, you can identify and direct campaigns towards your most valuable customer segments on Snapchat.
  * **Reactivating dormant users via Snapchat** : You can engage individuals who have shown reduced interest in your regular marketing efforts by delivering personalized display advertisements directly on Snapchat.
  * **Maximizing your Snapchat Ad budget** : You can refine your advertising expenditure by forming suppression lists within Snapchat Ads.
  * **Streamlining new customer growth on Snapchat** : You can build powerful lookalike audiences within the Snapchat Ads platform.

## Step 1: Create a Business Account on Snapchat

To set up a business account, perform the following steps:

  1. Log in to [Ads Manager](https://businesshelp.snapchat.com/s/article/set-up-snapchat?language=en_US#:~:text=in%20to%20Ads-,Manager,-using%20your%20preferred) using your preferred Snapchat username and password.
  2. Type your business name and click **Next**.
  3. Type your business details.
  4. Confirm your ad account country and currency, then add a phone number and business website.
  5. Click **Next**. This creates your organization and ad account.



After you have established a business, you can activate Marketing APIs and follow the steps below:

  1. Log in to [Ads Manager](https://ads.snapchat.com/).
  2. Click the menu in the upper-left corner and select **Business Dashboard** , then select **Business Details**.
  3. Click **+OAuth App**.
  4. Agree to the terms and conditions to activate access.
  5. Click **Save**.
  6. To connect MoEngage to your Snapchat App configuration, type the specific URL in the **Snap Redirect URI** box based on the following MoEngage data center:  
| Data Center |  Redirect URI

| --- | --- |  
| DC-01 | `https://dashboard-01.moengage.com/v3/partner/oauth/callback`  
| DC-02 |  `https://dashboard-02.moengage.com/v3/partner/oauth/callback`  
| DC-03 |  `https://dashboard-03.moengage.com/v3/partner/oauth/callback`  
| DC-04 |  `https://dashboard-04.moengage.com/v3/partner/oauth/callback`  
  
![](/images/partner_37557855812756.png)
  7. After your OAuth app is created, obtain the **Snap Client ID** and **Snap Client Secret Key**.  
![](/images/partner_37557855814804.png)

## Step 3: Create a Campaign on MoEngage to Sync Audiences to Snapchat Ads

To create a Snapchat campaign on MoEngage, perform the following steps:

  1. On the left navigation menu in the MoEngage dashboard, click **Engage** , and then click **Campaigns**.
  2. On the All campaigns page, click**\+ Create campaign**.
  3. On the **Create campaign** dialog box, under **Connected Apps** , click **Snapchat** and then click the most suitable delivery type. ![](/images/partner_38251623121940.png)
  4. Under **Select audience** , click **Filter users by**.
  5. On the **User property** tab, select the user segment for Snapchat syncing. You can combine this with other segment criteria.
  6. In step 2 of the campaign, **Content** , select the pre-configured connectors and specify the content you want to send out. Enter the following details:  

| Field | Description

| --- | --- |  
| **Connector** |  You can perform the following actions:
     * **Create Customer List.**
     * **Add Users to Customer List.**
     * **Remove Users from Customer List**.  
| **Connections** | Select the connection you’ve added to the MoEngage App marketplace.  
![](/images/partner_38251675507860.png)



### Sync to an Existing Audience on Snapchat

If you want to add or remove users from an existing Audience on Snapchat, you must provide the **Audience ID**. You can view the audience on Snapchat by navigating to [Snapchat Ads Manager](http://ads.snapchat.com/):

  1. Click the menu in the upper-left corner > **Assets** and select **Audiences**.
  2. Open your Audience by clicking the **Audience Name**.

  3. Note the Audience ID from the URL.




![](/images/partner_38369831839124.png)

### Create a New Audience on Snapchat

To create a new Audience on Snapchat, you can use the _Create Customer list_ connector and perform the following steps:

  1. Select the **Create a customer list** connector and the connection you created.
  2. Enter the **Audience Name** and other required details.
  3. Run a test campaign and review the response.![](/images/partner_38369831843860.png)The highlighted ID is your Audience ID. Use it to add or remove users from this Audience. You can find this ID in Snapchat here.
