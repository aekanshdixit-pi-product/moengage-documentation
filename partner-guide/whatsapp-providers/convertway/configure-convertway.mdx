MoEngage natively integrates with ConvertWay to send‬ WhatsApp templates‬‭ over Flows and campaigns directly‬‭ from MoEngage to end users. This‬ enhances customer engagement by leveraging WhatsApp as a communication channel for‬ automated messages and marketing campaigns.‬

Configuring ConvertWay with MoEngage is a two-step process:

Step 1: Sender Configuration

Step 2: Delivery Tracking

| library_add_check |  **Prerequisites** Before integration, ensure you have the following:

  * A MoEngage account
  * A ConvertWay (CW) account
  * A WhatsApp Business API (WABA) number

| --- | --- |  
  
# Sender Configuration

To configure a Sender from ConvertWay on the MoEngage Dashboard, follow the steps below:

  1. Go to **Settings** **>** **WhatsApp >** **Sender Configuration**.
  2. Click the **+Add connector** from the left pane under the **Sender configuration** tab.  


| info |  **Information** You can choose ConvertWay from the left pane if you have already added ConvertWay to your connector list.  
![image \(19\).png](/images/partner_35797513598740.png)

| --- | --- |  
  
  3. Select **ConvertWay **from the **Choose a connector** dropdown and then click **Done**.  
![Convertway.png](/images/partner_35797530059412.png)  


  4. Add the following details:

| Field Name | Description

| --- | --- |  
| Sender Name | 
     * Provide a name to the Sender profile.
     * This would be available in the Sender so that you can recognize this easily while using it to create a campaign inside MoEngage.
     * The **Sender Name** is a mandatory field that should be between 5-50 characters.  
| WhatsApp business number | 
     * Enter the phone number with the country code registered with WhatsApp (directly or through ConvertWay) using which you want to send out WhatsApp Messages to your users. 
     * In the ConvertWay account, you can find the sender details in **Settings** > **WhatsApp Business Account**.
     * This is a mandatory field and is also referred to as the WABA number.  
| API URL |  Collect the API URL from your vendor and enter it into the **API URL** field. Sample URL: **https://app.theconvertway.com/api/v1/send_messages/moengage/s‬**  
**‭ endWhatsapp‬**  
| Authorization |  **Basic CW_LICENSE_KEY** You can find the CW_LICENSE_KEY under **Settings** > **Profile** in‬  
‭ your ConvertWay account.  
  5. Click **Save**.
  6. Notify ConvertWay to enable/validate the API token.
  7. Upon successful integration, you get the following message.  
![SaveIntegration.png](/images/partner_35364754398740.png)
