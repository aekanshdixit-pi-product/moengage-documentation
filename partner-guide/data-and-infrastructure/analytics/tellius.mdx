# Introduction

[Tellius](https://www.tellius.com/) is an advanced analytics platform designed to help businesses of all sizes unlock the full value of their data and make informed decisions.

# MoEngage <> Tellius

The seamless integration between MoEngage and Tellius empowers users to unlock the full potential of their data, eliminating the need to allocate dedicated resources for BI. With this integration, you can effortlessly build dynamic dashboards and gain valuable insights to drive smarter marketing decisions. By storing MoEngage data in Snowflake, Tellius enables direct connectivity and real-time query execution, ensuring a seamless and efficient integration experience.

# Integration

| library_add_check |  **Prerequisites**

  * You need a Tellius account for this integration.
  * You will need to enable [Data Warehouses Exports](https://help.moengage.com/hc/en-us/sections/16100638998804-Data-warehouse) for your account.

| --- | --- |  
  
## Step 1: Forward data to your data warehouses

Using MoEngage's [Data Warehouses Exports](https://help.moengage.com/hc/en-us/sections/16100638998804-Data-warehouse), you can export your user's Campaign Interaction Data to your data warehouses like Snowflake, Redshift, BigQuery, etc. If you do not have these Exports enabled for your account, you can either speak to your Customer Success Manager or request enablement for these integrations directly from the MoEngage App Marketplace.

## Step 2: Connect your data warehouse to Tellius

When the MoEngage campaign interaction data starts flowing to your data warehouse, you can [load the datasets](https://help.tellius.com/article/jn6o59d5gk-load-datasets) to Tellius.

## Step 3: Create business views on Tellius

Once you have loaded the dataset, you can then proceed to create a Measure/Dimensions on top of the Campaign Interaction events. You can also create [Business Views](https://help.tellius.com/article/hy9yvh5tom-create-business-view) to start taking advantage of Tillius' natural language search and automated insights that will help you derive key information.
