# Introduction

[Airbyte](https://airbyte.com/) is an open-source data integration platform and ELT (Extract, Load, Transform) tool. It allows seamless integration, transformation, and loading of data with its powerful and user-friendly features.

# MoEngage × Airbyte

Exporting MoEngage campaign interactive events data to Airbyte allows you to centralize and store the data in a data warehouse. This enables data teams to perform comprehensive analysis, generate reports, and gain valuable insights into campaign performance, customer behavior, and engagement metrics.

## Step 1: Set up MoEngage Data Warehouses Exports

Using MoEngage's [Data Warehouses Exports](https://help.moengage.com/hc/en-us/sections/16100638998804-Data-warehouse), you can export your user's Campaign Interaction Data to your data warehouses like Snowflake, Redshift, BigQuery, etc. If you do not have these Exports enabled for your account, you can either speak to your Customer Success Manager or request enablement for these integrations directly from the MoEngage App Marketplace.

To view the format in which the data will be forwarded to your Data Warehouse, refer to the [Data Exports Glossary](https://help.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/15975781928468-Data-Export-Glossary).

## Step 2: Connect your Data Warehouse to your Airbyte account

  1. In your Airbyte Cloud account, go to _Sources >> \+ New Source > Set up the Source_.
  2. Select your Data Warehouse from the source dropdown.
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions to finish the configuration.



## Step 3: Connect your Destination

As per your requirements, select and configure your destination.
