# Introduction

[Boltic](https://www.boltic.io/) is an advanced data operations and automation platform that also allows you to define, track, and optimize your customer data across multiple channels by one of its feature Boltic streams. By integrating Boltic streams with MoEngage, you can seamlessly transfer your critical custom event and attribute data, ensuring that your customer engagement efforts are both targeted and effective. If you're already using Boltic to manage your data workflows, the Boltic streams - MoEngage Integration simplifies the process of sending event data to MoEngage. Boltic streams translates and routes your data in the format MoEngage understands, saving you valuable engineering time and effort, so you can focus on creating impactful campaigns.

# MoEngage × Boltic 

The Boltic and MoEngage integration enables you to track users and route data to MoEngage, enhancing your customer engagement strategies. Boltic provides the following capabilities:

  * **Custom Event Definition:** Define custom events within Boltic streams and seamlessly transfer them to MoEngage, ensuring that your campaigns are based on the most relevant customer interactions.
  * **Comprehensive Event Analysis:** Use Boltic’s event analysis dashboard to monitor and optimize event flows before they reach MoEngage, ensuring accuracy and relevance in your customer engagement.
  * **Seamless SDK Integration:** Boltic’s real-time debugger allows you to integrate the SDK smoothly, ensuring that only valid and correctly processed events are sent to MoEngage.
  * **Flexible Stream Control:** With Boltic, you have the flexibility to pause or resume streams and individual destinations, giving you full control over your data flow to MoEngage.
  * **Quick Setup with Pre-built Connectors:** Boltic’s pre-built connectors facilitate a fast and easy setup with MoEngage, allowing you to start leveraging customer data for personalized campaigns without delay.

<Info>

* Ensure you have access to the Settings >> APIs on your MoEngage dashboard
</Info>

## Step 1: Connect Boltic to your MoEngage account

  1. Enter a Destination Name by which you want to refer to this MoEngage Destination![Screenshot 2024-09-02 at 11.43.51 AM.png](/images/partner_33115695729428.png)
  2. Enter MoEngage dashboard URL![Screenshot 2024-09-02 at 11.48.09 AM.png](/images/partner_33115695729812.png)
  3. Enter your MoEngage Data API Key. You can access it from Settings >> APIs on your MoEngage dashboard  
![Screenshot 2024-09-02 at 11.48.37 AM.png](/images/partner_33115695730196.png)
  4. Enter your MoEngage Workspace ID. You can access it from Settings >> APIs on your MoEngage dashboard  
![Screenshot 2024-09-02 at 11.49.57 AM.png](/images/partner_33115664994580.png)
  5. Test and Save the destination. 



You have now successfully added MoEngage as destination to Boltic! Your defined events will start flowing to your account.

## Step 2: Validate data on MoEngage

You can search for the users from Segment >> Search users. The events will start flowing into your MoEngage account.
