# Introduction

<Info>
The Adobe integration does not currently support [Unified Identity (Identity Resolution)](/user-guide/data/user-data/unified-identity-identity-resolution) or [Portfolio](/user-guide/settings/account/portfolio/portfolio).
</Info>
[Adobe’s Real Time Customer Data Platform](https://business.adobe.com/in/products/real-time-customer-data-platform/RTCDP.html) helps companies bring together known and anonymous data from multiple enterprise sources to create customer profiles. These profiles can then be used to provide personalized experiences across all channels and devices in real-time.

[Click here to integrate your Adobe's RT CDP users and segments with MoEngage. ](/partner-guide/data-and-infrastructure/cdps/adobe-real-time-cdp/connections)
