</Info>

[mParticle](https://www.mparticle.com/)'s customer data platform empowers you to do more with your data. Sophisticated marketers use mParticle to orchestrate data across their entire growth stack, enabling them to win in key customer journey moments.

## MoEngage × mParticle

The MoEngage and mParticle integration allows you to seamlessly control the flow of information between the two systems:

- Sync mParticle audiences to MoEngage for campaigns and flows.
- Sync users and events from mParticle to MoEngage via server-to-server integration.
- Send MoEngage campaign interaction data to mParticle through Streams, making it actionable across your entire growth stack.

<Info>
**Prerequisites**
- Ensure you have an mParticle account.
- Ensure you have access to your MoEngage Dashboard Settings.
</Info>

## Integration scope

| Integration scope | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Sync mParticle audiences to MoEngage | [mParticle Audience](/partner-guide/data-and-infrastructure/cdps/mparticle/getting-started/mparticle-audience) |

| Send MoEngage campaign interaction data back to mParticle | [mParticle Inbound](/partner-guide/data-and-infrastructure/cdps/mparticle/getting-started/inbound-feed) |
