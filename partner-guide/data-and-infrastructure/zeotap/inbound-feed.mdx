# Introduction  
  
The MoEngage Events Stream integration allows you to send a continuous, real-time stream of your MoEngage engagement events directly into the Zeotap platform. After the data is in Zeotap, it is mapped to a standard catalog and can be used within Zeotap's activation modules, such as for building audiences or triggering journeys.<Info>
**Prerequisites**
This is a part of the Streams add-on. Please reach out to your dedicated MoEngage CSM (Customer Success Manager) to enable it for your account.
</Info>## Step 1: Add MoEngage as a Source on Zeotap

To send MoEngage campaign or event data to Zeotap, you must generate credentials in your Zeotap account and share them with the MoEngage team.

## Step 1.1: Get Your Zeotap Webhook and Write Key

To get the ZeoTap webhook and write key, perform the following steps:

  1. In your Zeotap dashboard, navigate to **Integrate** > **Sources**.

  2. Click **\+ Create Source** in the upper-right corner.  
![](/images/partner_42011021491732.png)  
The **CREATE SOURCE** dialog box appears.

  3. In the **Select Category** list, click **Customer Engagement Channels**.  
![](/images/partner_42010992240404.png)
  4. In the **SELECT DATA SOURCE** list, find and click **MoEngage Events stream**.  
![](/images/partner_42010992240660.png)  
The **CREATE SOURCE** dialog box appears.  

     1. In the **Source Name** box, enter a descriptive name.
     2. Under **Select your Region** , select and check the appropriate region for your account.

     3. Click **Create Source**.  
![](/images/partner_42011021492116.png)  
You will navigate back to the**Sources** page.
  5. Find and click the source name you created.
  6. Click the **IMPLEMENTATION DETAILS** tab.
  7. Under **Access Details** , copy the **API URL** (Webhook URL) and the **WRITE KEY** to provide to the MoEngage support team.![](/images/partner_42011021492244.png)



## Step 1.2: Activate MoEngage Streams

After you have your ZeoTap **API URL** (Webhook URL) and the **WRITE KEY** , contact your MoEngage Customer Success Manager (CSM) to activate MoEngage Streams. 

## Step 2: View Your Data in Zeotap

To preview the incoming data in real time, perform the following steps:

  1. Log in to your Zeotap dashboard, navigate to**Integrate** > **Sources**.
  2. On the **Sources** page, click the **PREVIEW DATA** tab.
  3. Click the data source that you want to preview.



![](/images/partner_42010992240916.png)  
You can now view the latest events and attributes received from MoEngage. The view includes columns such as event_name, uid, event_type, and various flattened user and event attributes.

## Activation and Data Usage

This establishes a secure, server-to-server connection, ensuring your event data flows reliably and safely from MoEngage to Zeotap. You can now leverage this real-time, trusted data within Zeotap to build powerful audiences and trigger personalized journeys.
