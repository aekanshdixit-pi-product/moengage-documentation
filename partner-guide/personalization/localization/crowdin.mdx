# Introduction

[Crowdin](https://crowdin.com/) is a cloud-based software for localization management. Crowdin helps in streamlining the localization of your app's user interface, website, marketing, and support content. This can help you deliver the content in multiple languages.

# MoEngage <> Crowdin

The MoEngage and Crowdin integration allows you to translate email templates and content blocks. You can also synchronize content/creatives from your MoEngage account to your Crowdin project and add translations back to MoEngage.

## Step 2: Share content between Crowdin and MoEngage

### A. Send translation from MoEngage to Crowdin 

To import content for translation from MoEngage:

  1. Open the MoEngage integration.
  2. In the MoEngage section (right panel), select the files that should be translated.
  3. Click **Sync**.



### B. Send translation from Crowdin to MoEngage 

  1. Open the MoEngage integration.
  2. In the left section, select the files that will be synced to MoEngage.
  3. Click **Sync to MoEngage**.



![](https://developer.app.crowdin.net/assets/4e814fb1-8662-4064-b7e0-6cce176288b5)

## Automatic sync

To automatically import content for translation from MoEngage:

Choose files and click **Scheduled sync** in the right panel in the MoEngage integration. The file you chose will be pushed once at a scheduled time (by default, it is done once a day). The same button is used to export translated files from Crowdin to MoEngage (in the left panel). You may disable this by clicking **Disable Sync**.

## Step 3 - Using translation in MoEngage campaigns

### Email templates- 

Once the templates are created, you will be able to access them in Step 2 of your Email Campaigns. You will see the newly created templates under the _My saved templates_ section.

[Click here to know more](https://help.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/360059171891-Create-Email-Campaign) about using templates in Email Campaigns. 

### Content blocks- 

Once the content blocks are created, you will be able to access them from **Content** >**Content blocks**. [Click here to know more](https://help.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/4414768369172-Content-blocks-) about using content blocks in MoEngage.
