# Introduction

[Quickads](https://www.quickads.ai/) is an AI-Ad Generator that creates effortless ads for all your campaigns.

# MoEngage × QuickAds

The integration between MoEngage and QuickAds allows customers to generate AI ads using QuickAds and send them via emails on MoEngage. This integration empowers businesses to enhance their marketing efforts by combining the creativity of QuickAds with the targeted reach of MoEngage's email marketing platform.

# Integration

<Info>
**Prerequisites**

* You need a Quickads account for this integration.
</Info>

## Step 1: Generate your ads on QuickAds

Using QuickAds, you can quickly define your brand color, and generate multiple high-quality ads in various formats:

  1. On the QuickAds dashboard, start the ad creation process.
  2. Describe what your ad is about, select your template, and finally, customize the colors accordingly.
  3. Once your ad is ready, you can further edit the Ad according to your use cases.
  4. Download your ad locally.



Read more about [creating ads](https://www.quickads.ai/tutorials) with QuickAds.

## Step 2: Use the ad inside MoEngage Campaigns

Once you have the image with you, you're all set to send this ad to your customers.

  1. Create a new [Email Campaign](https://help.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/360059171891-Create-Email-Campaign) in MoEngage.
  2. Select your template, and upload your image in the WYSIWYG editor.
  3. That's it, you're all set - you can test your campaign in Step 2.
  4. If you're satisfied with the email, you can now publish the campaign.
