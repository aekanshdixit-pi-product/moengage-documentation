# Introduction

[Wove](https://wove.com/) makes it possible to reach the audiences of brands complementary to yours. 

# MoEngage × Wove

With the MoEngage and Wove integration, you can include a Wove code snippet in your MoEngage email templates, and your readers will see messages from your Wove network partners—who can display your message to their audience in the same way..

# Integration

<Info>
**Prerequisites**

* Access to your Wove dashboard.
</Info>

## Step 1: Create your element on Wove dashabord

Go to the Wove dashboard and

  1. Create and customize a tag for each of your email templates that will include Wove partner messaging   
![Creating a custom tag in Wove](https://support.iterable.com/hc/article_attachments/360017364071/wove-1.png)
  2. After creating each tag, select Copy to ESP and click the copy symbol in the bottom-right corner.  
![Copy to ESP button](https://support.iterable.com/hc/article_attachments/360017364511/wove-snippet.png)  
  
You will need to paste it inside your MoEngage Campaign's Template.



## Step 2: Use this code inside MoEngage Campaigns

To include the Wove code in your MoEngage campaign, on step 2 of the campaign creation- 

![](/images/partner_24084255705364.png)

  1. Create a new HTML block
  2. Paste the copied code from Step 2 inside HTML Block. You can also insert the HTML code. 



You can choose to view the Preview by clicking on the Preview icon or test your campaign. Once completed, your Wove account manager will test the implementation and reach out with approval to go live.
