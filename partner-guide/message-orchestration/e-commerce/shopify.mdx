# Introduction

[Shopify](https://www.shopify.com/) is a leading global commerce company providing trusted tools to start, grow, market, and manage a retail business of any size. Shopify makes commerce better for everyone with its platform and services engineered for reliability while delivering a better shopping experience for consumers everywhere.

# MoEngage × Shopify

The Shopify-MoEngage integration offers a seamless way to integrate the MoEngage Web SDK into your brand's Shopify store, enabling you to track user events and data with ease. With this integration, you can leverage a variety of channels - including Web Push, Onsite Messaging, SMS, Email, and Web Personalization - to engage and nudge your store visitors toward conversion.

Read our [MoEngage × Shopify guide](/developer-guide/ecommerce-platforms/shopify/shopify-20) to start using the integration for your account.
