# Introduction

[Blitzllama](https://www.blitzllama.com/) GPT-powered feedback analytics automatically categorizes survey responses into topics, sentiments, and provides recommendations. 

# MoEngage × Blitzllama

The MoEngage and Blitzllama integration allows you to push your existing user segments from MoEngage to Blitzllama via MoEngage Connector Campaigns. Syncing cohorts into Blitzllama ensures you learn more about the same set of users.

# Integration

## Step 1: Create a connector campaign on MoEngage 

To create a connector campaign on MoEngage, 

1\. Navigate to Create Campaign >> Connectors >> Custom and choose delivery type most suitable to your needs ![Screenshot_2022-02-21_at_10.48.46_PM.png](/images/partner_30317159032980.png)

2\. On step 2 of the campaign 

  * Select method as POST
  * Add Webhook URL as : &lt;Your Blitzllama dashboard URL&gt;
  * In Headers, 
    * Content-Type as application/json
    * Add "x-api-key" with value &lt;to be fetched from your Blitzllama dashboard&gt;
  * In Body, choose Raw and copy paste the request. You can refer to the [blitzllama document](https://documentation.blitzllama.com/sdk/api) for reference 



3\. At this point, your campaign should be ready to test and send. You can also test your connector configuration using the Test functionality available on Step 2 of Create Connector Campaign. ![Screen_Shot_2022-02-22_at_11.12.42_AM.png](/images/partner_30317172449556.png)

If your test user successfully receives the message, your set-up is good to go. You can go ahead and Publish the campaign.

## Step 2: Use the Synced Cohorts on Blitzllama

As soon as your campaign is run, you should be able to see the segments (cohorts) on Blitzllama. These cohort can be used just like all other cohorts with the exception that these cannot be filtered further.  
  
In case you are stuck at any of the above steps, please don't hesitate to email us at tech@blitzllama.com or hit us up on the support chat.
