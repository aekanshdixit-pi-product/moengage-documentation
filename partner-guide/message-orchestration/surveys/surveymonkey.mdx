# Introduction

[SurveyMonkey](https://www.surveymonkey.com/) is a cloud-based survey tool that helps users create, send, and analyze surveys.

# MoEngage <> Survey Monkey

SurveyMonkey helps you to drive your business forward by using their online survey and forms tool to capture the voices and opinions of your users. With the MoEngage and SurveyMonkey integration, you can:

  * Imports MoEngage users to your SurveyMonkey Contacts.
  * Add your MoEngage users to your SurveyMonkey Contact Lists.



## Use Cases

Integrating Survey Monkey with MoEngage helps you with the following use cases:

  * **Targeted Feedback Loops** : Instead of blasting surveys to everyone, sync specific MoEngage segments (for example, "High Spenders" or "At-Risk Users") to SurveyMonkey to ask relevant questions.
  * **Demographic Synchronization** : Pre-fill survey fields (like Name, City, or Last Purchased Item) by passing MoEngage attributes to SurveyMonkey. This reduces friction for the user and improves survey completion rates.
  * **Closed-Loop Marketing** : (Requires Inbound Setup) Use survey responses to tag users in MoEngage. For example, if a user answers "I love Spicy Food" in a survey, update their MoEngage profile to trigger a "Spicy Food Deals" push notification.

### Call the SurveyMonkey API

To forward MoEngage users to SurveyMonkey, use the `POST` method and configure the payload.

  * Select the method as POST
  * Headers:
    * `Authorization`: `Bearer &lt;your_api_key&gt;`
    * `Content-Type`: `application/json`
  * Add the endpoint as Webhook URL: `https://api.surveymonkey.com/v3/contact_lists/&#123;&#123;CONTACT_LIST_ID&#125;&#125;/contacts` \- Replace `&#123;&#123;CONTACT_LIST_ID&#125;&#125;` with your contact list's ID.
  * Body:

JSON
        
        &#123;
            "first_name": "&#123;&#123;UserAttribute['First Name']&#125;&#125;",
            "last_name": "&#123;&#123;UserAttribute['Last Name']&#125;&#125;",
            "email": "&#123;&#123;UserAttribute['Email (Standard)']&#125;&#125;",
            "custom_fields": &#123;
                "Phone": "&#123;&#123;UserAttribute['Mobile Number (Standard)']&#125;&#125;",
                "City": "&#123;&#123;UserAttribute['Last Known City']&#125;&#125;",
                "Country": "&#123;&#123;UserAttribute['Last Known Country']&#125;&#125;"
