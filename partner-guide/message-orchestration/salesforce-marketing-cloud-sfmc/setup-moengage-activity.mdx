# Introduction

This guide provides detailed instructions for setting up MoEngage Custom Activities in Salesforce Marketing Cloud (SFMC). Follow these steps to ensure a smooth integration and usage.

| library_add_check |  **Prerequisites**

  * Salesforce Marketing Cloud Access: Administrator and Marketing Cloud Administrator permissions are required.
  * MoEngage API Credentials: Ensure you have access to your MoEngage APIs (Settings >> Accounts >> APIs)

| --- | --- |  
  
## Step 1: Set up MoEngage Custom Activity 

To set up the MoEngage custom activity, perform the following steps:

  1. Log in to your Salesforce Marketing Cloud (SFMC)  
![Screenshot 2025-04-14 at 1.35.43 PM.png](/images/partner_36576006441364.png)
  2. Access SFMC settings   
![Screenshot 2025-04-14 at 1.36.08 PM.png](/images/partner_36576006441620.png)
  3. Once you click on “Installed Packages”, this tab will be visible on the right side of the page   
![Screenshot 2025-04-14 at 1.37.40 PM.png](/images/partner_36576013645972.png)
  4. Create a new package   
![Screenshot 2025-04-14 at 1.38.52 PM.png](/images/partner_36576013646228.png)
  5. Add package details. You can name it "MoEngage Custom Activity" and add description as "Used to send one-time push notifications and run Event Trigger campaigns."   
![Screenshot 2025-04-14 at 1.39.29 PM.png](/images/partner_36576013646484.png)
  6. Add MoEngage Components  
![Screenshot 2025-04-14 at 1.42.39 PM.png](/images/partner_36576006447124.png)
  7. Click Add Component  
![Screenshot 2025-04-14 at 1.43.19 PM.png](/images/partner_36576006447636.png)
  8. Select “Journey Builder Activity” and click on “Next”.  
![Screenshot 2025-04-14 at 1.43.52 PM.png](/images/partner_36576006447764.png)
  9. Click Save. 
  10. Repeat the same steps for MoEngage Event Destination.   
![Screenshot 2025-04-14 at 1.46.13 PM.png](/images/partner_36576013650708.png)



## Step 2: Set up the Cloud Page and the Data Extension 

You need to set up a cloud page along with a data extension to capture the MoEngage workspace details. These can then be used while configuring a MoEngage destination in Journey Builder:

  1. Navigate to the SFMC Home page.
  2. Click “Platform” >> “Package Manager”.   
![Screenshot 2025-04-14 at 1.51.45 PM.png](/images/partner_36576006448404.png)  
Once you click on the “Package Manager”, it will be redirected to the Package Manager page.
  3. Click on the “Deployment” tab
  4. Click "Deploy"  
![Screenshot 2025-04-14 at 1.51.51 PM.png](/images/partner_36576013651476.png)
  5. Upload [JSON](https://partners.moengage.com/hc/en-us/article_attachments/45740967830932) .  
![Screenshot 2025-04-14 at 1.54.03 PM.png](/images/partner_36576013651860.png)[Optional] If applicable, select a “CloudPages Domain” and click “Next”.  
![Screenshot 2025-04-14 at 1.54.12 PM.png](/images/partner_36576006449428.png)
  6. The system will validate the setup and check for any issues. Once validated, click Deploy.   
![Screenshot 2025-04-14 at 1.56.55 PM.png](/images/partner_36576006449556.png)  


### Publish Data Extensions and CloudPages

  7. Go to the “Homepage”
  8. Click on “Web Studio”, and then click on “CloudPages”

  10. Click on the “arrow”to open the dropdown list and then click on “Edit”.  
![Screenshot 2025-04-14 at 2.01.08 PM.png](/images/partner_36576013658644.png)
  11. Click on “Save” and then on “Schedule/Publish”.  
![Screenshot 2025-04-14 at 2.01.47 PM.png](/images/partner_36576013658900.png)
  12. Return to the “CloudPages” page, click on “MoEngage Credentials” subfolder and then on “User Authentication Page”.
  13. Click on the “arrow”to open the dropdown list and then click on “Edit”  
![Screenshot 2025-04-14 at 2.03.00 PM.png](/images/partner_36576006451220.png)
  14. Click on “Save” and then on “Schedule/Publish”.  
![Screenshot 2025-04-14 at 2.03.07 PM.png](/images/partner_36576013659540.png)



## Verify Data Extension and External Keys

Ensure the following Data Extensions (DEs) are properly named and configured: 

### A. MoEngage Tracking Data

It stores campaign tracking information.

  1.      1. Go to the “Homepage”
     2. Click on “Audience Builder”, and then click on “Contact Builder”.  
![Screenshot 2025-04-14 at 2.05.15 PM.png](/images/partner_36576006452372.png)  

     3. Select “Data Extensions” tab  

     4. Click on “Data Extensions” folder, and then click on “MoEngage Data Track” subfolder. 
     5. Select “MoEngage Tracking data” extension.  
  
![Screenshot 2025-04-14 at 2.05.24 PM.png](/images/partner_36576013660052.png)Here you can find the “External Key”, please ensure it is accurate. [Optional] If external key needs to be entered or changed, click on “External Key” input field, paste or enter the external key and click on “Done”.



### B. MoEngage Credentials

It stores the credentials for the Data Tracking CloudPage:

  1.      1. Go to the “Homepage”
     2. Click on “Audience Builder”, and then click on “Contact Builder”.  
![Screenshot 2025-04-14 at 2.11.32 PM.png](/images/partner_36576013660564.png)  

     3. Select “Data Extensions” tab.
     4. Click on “Data Extensions” folder, and then click on “MoEngage Data Track” subfolder. 
     5. Select “MoEngage Tracking Credential”  
![Screenshot 2025-04-14 at 2.11.24 PM.png](/images/partner_36576013660820.png)  
Here you can find the “External Key”, please ensure it is accurate. [Optional] If external key needs to be entered or changed, click on “External Key” input field, paste or enter the external key and click on “Done”.

## Configure Data Extension  


### A. Configure Data Extensions:

  1. Change the external key of the credentials data extension from the solution package to “MoEngage Credentials".  
![](/images/partner_43091841852564.png)
  2. Change the external key of the “Sync Users to MoEngage” data extension from the solution package to “Sync Users to MoEngage”.![](/images/partner_43091889328020.png)
  3. Change the external key of the “User Sync Automation Logs” data extension from the solution package to “User Sync Automation Logs”.![](/images/partner_43091841854484.png)



## Connect your MoEngage Workspace

### A. Access the credentials cloud page 

On your SFMC dashboard, perform the following steps:

  1. Navigate to “Web Studio” >> “CloudPages” >> “MoEngage Credentials” subfolder. Finally click on “MoEngage Authentication Page”. Click Edit.
  2. Click on the “Page URL” to open the Authentication Page.   
![Screenshot 2025-04-14 at 2.22.17 PM.png](/images/partner_36576013661716.png)
  3. Enter details of your MoEngage workspace. And click Submit. 

| Field  | Value

| --- | --- |  
| Data center | Select your MoEngage data center. Refer to the guide here for details- [https://help.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/360057030512-Data-Centers-in-MoEngage](https://help.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/360057030512-Data-Centers-in-MoEngage)  
| Workspace Name  | Enter your workspace name. Its present in the header on your MoEngage dashboard.   
![Screenshot 2025-04-14 at 2.33.43 PM.png](/images/partner_36576013662100.png)  
| Workspace ID | Navigate to Settings >> Accounts >> APIs. ![Screenshot 2025-04-14 at 2.28.39 PM.png](/images/partner_36576006455316.png)
