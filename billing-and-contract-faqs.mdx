## Usage and Billable Metrics

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="How does MoEngage calculate billable usage (MTUs / MAUs)?">
  MoEngage measures usage through two metrics:

  - **Monthly Tracked Users (MTUs)** — Unique end-user profiles that perform at least one event in a calendar month.
  - **Monthly Active Users (MAUs)** — Unique users who interact with your app or website in a calendar month.

  Your bill is based on whichever metric is higher in a given month. This ensures your pricing reflects the metric that best captures your platform's reach and stays aligned with how your business is actually growing.
</Accordion>

## Billing Start and Invoicing

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="When does billing start after contract signing?">
  There are two separate billing events after signing:

  1. **Implementation / Onboarding fees** are invoiced immediately upon contract signing.
  2. **Platform Subscription fees** are invoiced on the Platform Subscription Start Date specified in your Order Form.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="We haven't completed go-live across all business units yet. How is billing handled?">
  Billing is independent of your integration progress. The Platform Subscription fee runs from the Start Date in your Order Form, regardless of whether you have gone live across all business units.

  <Tip>
  Partner closely with your CSM during onboarding so your team can begin realizing value as quickly as possible.
  </Tip>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How is an overage charge calculated when my usage exceeds the contracted limit?">
  If your usage exceeds your contracted limit — whether for MTUs or channel-based usage like SMS, Email, or Push — an overage invoice is automatically generated.

  Overages are billed in fixed blocks rather than one unit at a time. Once your usage crosses into the next block, you are charged for the full block, so you always have headroom to keep running campaigns without interruption.

  <Tip>
  If you're approaching your limit and want to review your plan or upgrade your allocation, reach out to your Customer Success Manager.
  </Tip>
</Accordion>

## Annual Escalations

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="How and when are annual price increase applied?">
  As per the escalation clause, an increase in price is applied at each anniversary of your Platform Subscription Start Date. The specific rate for your account is stated in your Order Form.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Does the annual price increment still apply if a later addendum does not mention it?">
  Yes. The escalation clause in your Order Form, or in any previously signed addendum, remains in force unless a subsequent addendum explicitly supersedes or waives it.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Where can I find the annual escalation clause?">
  You can find it in your Order Form Terms or your latest signed contract. If you'd like a walkthrough, your CSM can take you through the relevant clauses.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Subscription Changes

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="How do I stop being billed for a product or service I no longer use?">
  Reach out to your Customer Success Manager. They will guide you through executing an addendum to formally remove the product or service from your subscription.

  <Warning>
  Simply pausing usage does not stop billing. A signed contract change is required.
  </Warning>
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
