## Getting Started

New to MoEngage SDKs? Here's a quick guide to get up and running:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your platform">
    Select the SDK that matches your application's platform from the cards above.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install the SDK">
    Follow the SDK Integration guide for your chosen platform to add the MoEngage dependency.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initialize MoEngage">
    Configure your MoEngage App ID and initialize the SDK in your application code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up push notifications">
    Configure push notification certificates (APNs for iOS, FCM for Android) and enable push in your app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track user data">
    Start tracking user attributes and events to power segmentation and personalized campaigns.
  </Step>
</Steps>
