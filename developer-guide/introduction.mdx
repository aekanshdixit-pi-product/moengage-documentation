Your central hub for integrating MoEngage into your applications. Pick your platform, follow the integration guide, and start engaging your users with push notifications, in-app messages, analytics, and more.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your platform">
    Pick the SDK that matches your app's platform from the sections below.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install the SDK">
    Follow the integration guide for your platform to add MoEngage as a dependency.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initialize MoEngage">
    Configure your MoEngage App ID and initialize the SDK in your application code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track users and events">
    Start tracking user attributes and custom events to power segmentation and campaigns.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable engagement channels">
    Set up push notifications, in-app messages, or cards to begin engaging users.
  </Step>
</Steps>
