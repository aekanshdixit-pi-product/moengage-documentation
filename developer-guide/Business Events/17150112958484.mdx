Business Events include product launches, new episode releases for an OTT series, travel delays (flight delays), price drops in E-Commerce platforms, and so on. The Business Event APIs help you create Business Events, trigger them and search for them in MoEngage.

The following APIs are available: 

* [Create Business Event API](https://developers.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/17150140172180-Create-Business-Event-API)
* [Trigger Business Event API](https://developers.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/17150140786964-Trigger-Business-Event-API)
* [Search Business Event API](https://developers.moengage.com/hc/en-us/articles/17150130477204-Search-Business-Event-API)
